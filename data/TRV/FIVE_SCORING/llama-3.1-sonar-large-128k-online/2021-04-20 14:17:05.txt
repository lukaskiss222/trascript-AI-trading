6. **M&A Strategy**: There is no change in Travelers' cautious and strategic approach to M&A, focusing on deals that improve long-term returns, reduce volatility, or create shareholder value. This does not indicate any immediate or significant M&A activity[4].

- The ongoing challenges in investment yields and the impact of catastrophe losses are somewhat offset by the company's robust underwriting and capital management strategies.
