Travelers’ Q3 2010 earnings showcased robust financial health with a 12% year-over-year earnings increase, a strong combined ratio, and book value growth. However, concerns about low investment returns, potential sustained low yield environment, and their impact on future returns introduce uncertainty. The company’s strategic focus on retention, selective pricing, and capital management, along with its proactive approach to managing municipal bond exposure, should provide some near-term stability to the stock price.
