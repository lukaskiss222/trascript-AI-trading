The Travelers Companies, Inc. reported strong first-quarter results with net income of $806 million and a 13.1% return on equity. The company achieved significant improvements in pricing and retention across its Business Insurance segment, with renewal premium change of 9% and retention levels remaining stable. The Financial, Professional & International Insurance segment also performed well, with operating income up 24% year-over-year. The Personal Insurance segment saw improved pricing and retention in automobile and homeowners lines. Management highlighted the company's commitment to returning excess capital to shareholders through dividends and share repurchases. The quarterly dividend was increased by 12%, bringing the annual yield to 3.1%. The company remains focused on improving profitability through active pricing and improved terms and conditions. Overall, the earnings report and management commentary indicate a strong start to the year, with the company well-positioned for continued growth.
