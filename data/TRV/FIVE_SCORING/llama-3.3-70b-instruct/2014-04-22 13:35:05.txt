The Travelers Companies, Inc. reported an outstanding start to 2014 with record net and operating income per diluted share, driven by strong underwriting performance and solid investment results. Despite a challenging winter weather, the company achieved a combined ratio of 85.7% and an operating return on equity of 17.8%. Management's guidance for the year remains unchanged, with an expectation of achieving a mid-teens return on equity over time. The company's focus on improving returns through granular pricing, risk selection, and expense management is yielding positive results. The rollout of Quantum Auto 2.0 is also showing promising signs, with improved new business production and retention. Overall, the company's strong performance and strategic initiatives position it well for long-term success.
