The Travelers Companies, Inc. reported a strong performance for the fourth quarter and full year 2008, despite the challenging economic environment. The company's operating return on equity (ROE) was 12.4% for the quarter and 14.5% for the full year, demonstrating its ability to manage risks and achieve targeted results. Travelers' management expressed confidence in the company's position and strategy, highlighting its disciplined underwriting approach, diversified portfolio, and strong investment portfolio. The company's guidance for 2009 projects an operating ROE of 10% to 11%, which, although lower than the previous year, is still a respectable result considering the current economic conditions. Overall, Travelers' financials and management's tone suggest a positive outlook for the company.
