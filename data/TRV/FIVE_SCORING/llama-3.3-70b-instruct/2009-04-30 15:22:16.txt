The Travelers Companies, Inc. reported solid Q1 results, with operating income of $799 million and a combined ratio of 93.9%. The company's business insurance segment saw improved pricing trends, with rates approaching zero, and an increase in new business writings. The personal insurance segment also performed well, with a combined ratio of 93% and 3% growth in net written premiums. Travelers' direct-to-consumer initiative is expected to impact the combined ratio by 2.5 points for auto and 1 point for homeowner and other in 2009. The company's investment portfolio remains in good shape, with a high-quality $72 billion portfolio and low impairments. Management expressed confidence in the company's ability to succeed in the direct channel and is focused on long-term growth and profitability.
