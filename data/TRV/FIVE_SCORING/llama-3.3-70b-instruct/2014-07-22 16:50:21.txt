The Travelers Companies, Inc. reported strong Q2 results, with operating income of $673 million and a combined ratio of 96.5%. The company's management expressed confidence in their ability to continue generating strong returns, driven by their focus on underwriting discipline and expansion into new markets. While the industry is experiencing some pressure on pricing, Travelers' management believes that their strong market position and diversified portfolio will enable them to maintain profitability. The company also announced a significant increase in their dividend payout and share buyback program, demonstrating their commitment to returning capital to shareholders.
