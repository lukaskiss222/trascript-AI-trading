The Travelers Companies reported a strong performance for the third quarter of 2009, with operating income of $914 million and an increased quarterly dividend by 10%. With guidance raised for full-year earnings, the stock is likely to experience a positive impact in the short term as investor sentiment aligns with optimistic financial outlook and continued capital return.
