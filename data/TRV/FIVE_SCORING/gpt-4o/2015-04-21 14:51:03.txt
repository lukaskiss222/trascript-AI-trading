The Travelers Companies reported a strong first quarter for 2015, with significant profitability and high retention rates, while also increasing its dividend and authorizing an additional $5 billion for share repurchases. The firm demonstrated stable underwriting margins despite challenging weather conditions, and management communicated confidence in their strategic initiatives. Although investment income was reduced, it seems this was mostly anticipated, and other segments of the business demonstrated resilience. Overall, the market is likely to react positively to the company's continued strong performance and proactive capital management strategies.
