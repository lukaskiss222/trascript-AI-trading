The Travelers Companies' earnings call highlights strong Q4 and full-year 2014 results, driven by record net income and ROE.  Management emphasized stable market conditions across their businesses, particularly in commercial insurance.  While some minor negative reserve developments (mostly in personal auto) were noted, overall results were positive and the company remains optimistic about continued shareholder value creation, with significant capital returned to investors to date.  Short-term impacts are likely to be positive, as the company's strong performance and guidance should keep investors bullish in the coming weeks.
