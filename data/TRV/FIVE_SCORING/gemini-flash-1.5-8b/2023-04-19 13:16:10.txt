The Travelers Companies reported strong Q1 2023 earnings, exceeding expectations with 12% net written premium growth.  Management highlighted strong performance across all segments, particularly Business Insurance, and the return of $680 million to shareholders.  Despite elevated catastrophe losses, the company maintained profitability.  The outlook suggests continued pricing pressure and potential further rate increases in certain lines to combat inflationary loss trends, impacting the short-term stock price positively.
