The Travelers Companies reported record core income per diluted share and net written premiums, exceeding expectations.  Management highlighted strong pricing and retention across all segments, suggesting a bullish outlook for premiums.  However, the challenging tort environment continued to negatively impact underwriting results, and they expect that to continue.  The outlook anticipates improved results in the second half of 2020 but notes continued uncertainty given the ongoing impact of the tort environment.
