The Travelers Companies delivered strong Q1 2013 earnings, exceeding expectations with record operating income per share and significant margin improvements.  Management highlights strong pricing and underwriting actions as key drivers of profitability.  However, reduced new business growth in Personal Insurance, despite strong renewal rates, suggests potential headwinds from pricing strategies.  The stock should see positive short-term impact from strong earnings but tempered growth expectations could create some pressure around new business activity.
