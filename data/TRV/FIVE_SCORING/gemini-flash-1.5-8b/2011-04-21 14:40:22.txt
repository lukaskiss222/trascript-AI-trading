The Travelers Companies' Q1 2011 earnings call highlighted strong operating results, driven by lower-than-expected catastrophe losses and strategic pricing actions. Management expressed confidence in their pricing strategy, noting improved rate and retention in multiple business segments, particularly commercial lines. While acknowledging some headwinds in the surety market, they emphasize a cautious optimism for future performance, projecting continued positive trends in the coming quarters.  This positive outlook, coupled with share buybacks and dividend increases, suggests a strong positive short-term impact on the stock price.
