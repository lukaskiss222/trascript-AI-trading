The Travelers Companies' earnings call highlights strong Q2 2021 performance, exceeding expectations across underwriting and investment segments.  Management emphasizes continued strong pricing and retention in all segments, particularly Business Insurance.  While acknowledging some inflationary pressures, especially in personal auto, the company's focus on pricing and risk management suggest a positive outlook for the next few weeks, underpinned by their ability to react to short-term claim severity increases.
