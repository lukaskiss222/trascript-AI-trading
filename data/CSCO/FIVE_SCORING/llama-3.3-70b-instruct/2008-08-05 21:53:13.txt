Cisco's Q4 earnings call revealed a mixed bag of results, with the company beating revenue expectations but guiding for slower growth in the next quarter. While the company's emerging markets and advanced technologies segments showed strong growth, the service provider segment was a concern due to mixed signals in capital expenditure spending.
