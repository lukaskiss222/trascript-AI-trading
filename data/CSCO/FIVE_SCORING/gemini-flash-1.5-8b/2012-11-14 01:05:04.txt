Cisco's Q1 FY 2013 earnings call highlighted some positive trends, notably strong U.S. enterprise and service provider growth, exceeding guidance on profitability with double-digit EPS growth, and consistent service business growth. While Europe remains a concern, management indicated a desire to become the leading IT company, positioning themselves for future market trends. The call suggests a short-term positive impact, driven by success in key segments and management confidence in their strategy.
