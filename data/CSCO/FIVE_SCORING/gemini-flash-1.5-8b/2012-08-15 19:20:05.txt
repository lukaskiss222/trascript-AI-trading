Cisco reported strong Q4 and FY2012 results, exceeding expectations with record earnings per share growth and revenue.  Management highlighted the successful execution of Cisco's strategy, particularly in the data center and service provider markets, and the positive impact of the London Olympics.  While acknowledging challenges in EMEA and some slowing in certain markets, their proactive guidance, focus on emerging markets, and strategic partnerships suggest a positive trajectory for the next few weeks, driven by a combination of strong demand and market positioning in a tough environment.
