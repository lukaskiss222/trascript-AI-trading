The earnings transcript for Cisco Systems, Inc. for the first quarter of fiscal year 2007 indicates a strong performance across various metrics. Here are the key points:

- Advanced Technologies, including wireless, unified communications, and networked home, showed significant growth[3].

## Guidance and Outlook
- Guidance for Q2 fiscal 2007 is for year-over-year revenue growth of 24% to 25%, with Cisco standalone growth expected at 14% to 15%[3].
