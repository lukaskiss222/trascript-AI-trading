### Analysis

Cisco Systems reported strong financial results for Q4 and FY 2023, with record revenue, earnings per share, and operating cash flow. The company achieved double-digit growth in most customer segments, with notable improvements in enterprise networking and security. Management provided guidance for FY 2024, projecting modest revenue growth and faster bottom-line growth, driven by operating leverage and strong recurring revenue.

### Key Points

1. **Record Sales and Earnings**: Cisco reported $15.2 billion in Q4 revenue, up 16% year-over-year, and $4.7 billion in non-GAAP net income, up 36%. The company also achieved record operating cash flow of $6 billion.
2. **Product and Service Revenue**: Product revenue was $11.7 billion, up 20%, while service revenue was $3.6 billion, up 4%. Secure Agile Networks and wireless LAN were the strongest performers.
3. **Software and Recurring Revenue**: Cisco's software revenue accelerated to $4.6 billion, up 17%, with 85% of software revenue being subscription-based. Total subscription revenue was $6.6 billion, an increase of 13%.
4. **Guidance for FY 2024**: Cisco expects revenue to be in the range of $57 billion to $58.2 billion, with non-GAAP earnings per share expected to range from $4.01 to $4.08. The company also expects non-GAAP gross margin to be in the range of 65% to 66% and non-GAAP operating margin to be in the range of 34% to 35%.
5. **Capital Allocation**: Cisco committed to increasing shareholder returns through greater operating leverage, increased share repurchases, and growing its dividend. The company returned $2.8 billion to shareholders in Q4, including $1.6 billion for its quarterly cash dividend and $1.3 billion of share repurchases.

### Conclusion

Cisco's strong Q4 and FY 2023 results, along with its optimistic guidance for FY 2024, suggest that the company is well-positioned to continue growing its business. The company's focus on innovation, recurring revenue, and capital allocation to shareholders indicates that it is committed to delivering value to its investors. The stock is likely to see a positive impact in the short term due to these factors.
