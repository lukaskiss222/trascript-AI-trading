Cisco reported strong Q3 results, exceeding revenue and earnings expectations with a 21% year-over-year revenue increase and record non-GAAP EPS of $0.34. The company raised its Q4 revenue guidance to 15%-16% growth, above its long-term target, reflecting confidence in ongoing momentum. Positive performance across products and geographies, along with optimistic guidance, is likely to have a positive impact on the stock price in the short term.
