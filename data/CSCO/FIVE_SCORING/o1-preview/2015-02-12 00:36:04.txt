Cisco reported strong quarterly results, with revenue up 7% and EPS exceeding expectations by growing 13% year-over-year. The company showed robust growth in key segments like switching (up 11%) and data center (up 40%), and increased its dividend by 11%, reflecting confidence in future performance. Despite some challenges in emerging markets and the service provider segment, the positive results and optimistic management tone are likely to have a favorable impact on the stock price in the short term.
