Microsoft's Q2 results showed solid financial performance despite a challenging PC market. The strong demand for business products and services, along with a record holiday season driven by Xbox, was encouraging. However, the PC market softness and muted consumer spending kept overall growth modest. The upcoming Windows 8 release and strong enterprise demand are positive signals. But the stock is likely to remain steady, awaiting concrete evidence of Windows 8's success and a significant shift in consumer spending.
