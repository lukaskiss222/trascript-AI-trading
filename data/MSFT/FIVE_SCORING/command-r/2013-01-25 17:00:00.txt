Microsoft's Q2 2013 earnings call reveals a solid financial performance with record revenue and earnings per share. However, the focus is on the recent Windows 8 launch and its impact on the ecosystem. The mixed response to Windows 8 and high price points of touch devices are acknowledged, but Microsoft remains optimistic about the ecosystem's growth potential. The stock is likely to face some pressure due to the challenges in the Windows ecosystem, but the long-term potential of Windows 8 and Microsoft's diverse product portfolio offer hope.
