Microsoft's Q1 earnings call revealed strong performance across all divisions, exceeding expectations with 27% revenue growth and 32% operating income growth. Management's tone was positive, citing a strong PC hardware market, healthy demand from businesses, and a successful launch of Halo 3. Guidance for the year was increased, with revenue growth expected to be 15% to 17%. The stock is likely to experience a positive impact in the short-term due to the company's strong financial performance and increased guidance.
