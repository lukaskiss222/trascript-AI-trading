Microsoft's Q2 earnings call revealed a strong quarter with record revenues, driven by growth in core software businesses and successful product launches, including XBOX 360 and SQL Server 2005. Despite supply constraints affecting XBOX 360 sales, the company remains confident in its ability to meet demand and achieve guidance for the fiscal year. Additionally, Microsoft is investing heavily in MSN and Ad Center to drive long-term growth in search and online advertising. The company's guidance for Q3 and the full fiscal year indicates continued double-digit revenue and earnings growth, driven by strength in core businesses and new product launches.
