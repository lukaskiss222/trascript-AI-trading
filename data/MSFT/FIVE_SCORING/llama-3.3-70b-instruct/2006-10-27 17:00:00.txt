Microsoft's Q1 FY07 earnings call revealed a strong start to the fiscal year, with revenue growth of 11% and EPS exceeding expectations. The company anticipates double-digit revenue growth for the full year, driven by product launches and a strong PC market. While there are concerns around the online services business, management expressed confidence in the division's progress and potential for growth. Overall, the company's financial performance and guidance suggest a positive short-term outlook.
