Microsoft's Q3 2018 earnings call highlighted several positive trends and strong performance metrics, which are likely to have a positive impact on the stock price in the short term.

### Key Points:
- **Revenue and Profit Growth**: Microsoft reported a 16% increase in revenue, a 23% increase in operating income, and a 35% increase in net income, all of which exceeded expectations[2][3][5].
- **Cloud Performance**: Azure revenue grew by 93%, and commercial cloud revenue increased by 58%, indicating strong growth in Microsoft's cloud services[2][3][5].
- **Segment Performance**: All segments, including Productivity and Business Processes, Intelligent Cloud, and More Personal Computing, showed significant growth. Notably, Dynamics 365 and LinkedIn also saw substantial revenue increases[2][3][5].
- **Guidance**: The company provided strong guidance for Q4, expecting continued growth in commercial cloud revenue, server products, and gaming[2][3][5].
- **Management Tone**: Both Satya Nadella and Amy Hood expressed confidence in the company's performance and future growth opportunities, highlighting innovation, customer engagement, and strategic investments[2][3][5].

### Short-Term Impact:
Given the robust financial performance, positive guidance, and the management's optimistic tone, the stock is likely to see a positive reaction in the short term.
