The company demonstrated strong momentum with:
- Accelerating revenue growth of 13% year-over-year
- Strong performance across core businesses, particularly Server & Tools (16% growth)

- Record Xbox 360 sales and supply chain improvements

While there are some concerns about increased investments impacting margins in FY07, the near-term outlook remains robust with Q4 revenue guidance of 13-15% growth and continued strength across major product lines.
