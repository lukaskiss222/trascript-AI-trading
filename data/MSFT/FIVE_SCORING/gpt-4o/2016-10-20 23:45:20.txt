In the Microsoft Corporation earnings call for Q1 FY 2017, the company reported $22.3 billion in revenue, which represented a 5% increase in constant currency. Key highlights include strong growth in Office 365 and Azure, with Office 365 commercial users surpassing 85 million and Azure usage more than doubling year-over-year. Significant margin improvements were also noted in Microsoft's commercial cloud segment, which reflects the company's focus on delivering higher-level services rather than just commodity services. The management's tone was positive and confident, emphasizing strong execution and market position in AI, cloud, and other growth areas. In the short term, the stock is likely to experience a positive impact driven by these impressive growth figures and successful execution against strategic initiatives.
