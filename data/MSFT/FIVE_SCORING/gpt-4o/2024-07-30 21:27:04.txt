Microsoft has delivered another strong quarter with notable revenue growth, particularly in its cloud segment fueled by its AI offerings and Azure services. The management's tone was confident, emphasizing sustained momentum across product segments, including solid demand for AI-driven solutions such as M365 Copilot.
