Microsoft's Q3 2019 earnings call highlighted strong double-digit growth across various segments, exceeding expectations in many areas.  Management emphasized continued momentum in cloud adoption, particularly with Azure, and strong performance in Office 365, with positive commentary on the evolving mix towards E5 subscriptions and increased first-line integrations.  The continued focus on hybrid and cloud solutions, coupled with optimistic guidance for Q4, suggests a positive short-term outlook for the stock.
