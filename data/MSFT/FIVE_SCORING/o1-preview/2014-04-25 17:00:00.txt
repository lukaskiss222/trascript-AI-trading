Microsoft's Q3 earnings call showcased strong financial performance, with revenue up 8% to $20.4 billion and earnings per share growing by 5%. The company reported exceptional growth in its cloud services, with Commercial Cloud revenue more than doubling, Office 365 reaching a $2.5 billion annual run rate, and Azure revenue increasing over 150%. Management's optimistic tone, emphasis on innovation in a mobile-first, cloud-first world, and positive guidance point to a likely positive short-term impact on the stock price.
