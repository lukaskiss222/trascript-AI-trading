McDonald's earnings call in April 2015 revealed significant challenges, primarily negative comparable sales across most regions, particularly APMEA (with Japan a major concern).  Management highlighted a need for a turnaround plan, including streamlining operations, simplifying menus, and prioritizing customer-centric strategies.  While some positive trends exist in Australia, the UK, and Canada, the overall tone suggests a cautious outlook for the short term, with significant uncertainty surrounding 2015 guidance.
