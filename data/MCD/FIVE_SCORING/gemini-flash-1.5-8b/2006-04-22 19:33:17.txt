McDonald's Q1 earnings call highlighted strong comparable sales growth, driven by increased guest counts and improved margins across all segments.  Management emphasized a successful "plan to win," citing the alignment of operators, employees, and suppliers; a robust product pipeline; and positive performance in key markets like France, Asia-Pacific, and Latin America.  While acknowledging some challenges in the UK, the call overall presents a positive outlook for the company's short-term performance.
