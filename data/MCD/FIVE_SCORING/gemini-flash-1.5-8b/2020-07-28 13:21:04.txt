McDonald's Q2 2020 earnings call highlights a return to profitability, driven by strong drive-thru sales and pent-up demand as markets reopened.  While breakfast sales remain a challenge, the company emphasizes the resilience of its system, its solid financial position, and aggressive marketing plans to capitalize on market share gains.  The call suggests the stock might see a short-term rally, driven by the successful reopening and recovery.
