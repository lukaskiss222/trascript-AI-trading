McDonald's 2018 earnings call highlights significant momentum, with strong comparable sales growth, positive guest counts, and improved customer satisfaction.  Management emphasizes the success of the "Velocity Growth Plan" and substantial investments in both the US and internationally to further enhance the customer experience.  The tax impact of 2017 is fully disclosed and management expects some short-term choppiness in 2018 but remains optimistic about achieving strong, sustainable long-term growth.
