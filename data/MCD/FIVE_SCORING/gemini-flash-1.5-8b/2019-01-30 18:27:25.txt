McDonald's 2018 earnings transcript highlights continued growth momentum despite a muted informal dining environment.  Management emphasizes strong performance in international markets, particularly the UK, Germany, and Australia.  While the U.S. faces challenges, the company's "Experience of the Future" initiatives and new menu items show positive trends in average check amounts.  The outlook anticipates short-term pressure on EPS due to labor costs, EOTF depreciation, and foreign currency shifts but projects long-term profitability and shareholder returns.
