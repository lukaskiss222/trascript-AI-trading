McDonald's earnings call reveals a focus on short-term customer engagement and value initiatives, like the Dollar Menu and More, to combat sluggish comps.  Management emphasizes strengthening relationships with customers, particularly in key markets like Germany and Japan. While acknowledging macroeconomic headwinds, the company anticipates a relatively flat comparable sales performance and is emphasizing operational efficiency improvements, such as the new high-density kitchens.  These adjustments are expected to provide a short-term improvement in operational efficiency and customer experience.
