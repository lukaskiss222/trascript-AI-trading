McDonald's earnings call highlighted strong Q2 2006 performance, driven by global sales growth and margin improvements in all regions, especially Europe (with UK and Germany leading the charge).  Management emphasized continued focus on their 'Plan to Win' strategy, including new product introductions, operational efficiency improvements, and expansion of convenience offerings.  The company's emphasis on value, premium products, and evolving the customer dining experience suggest continued positive momentum for the next two weeks.
