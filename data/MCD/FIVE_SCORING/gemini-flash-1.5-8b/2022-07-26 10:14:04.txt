McDonald's Q2 2022 earnings show strong global comp sales growth despite a challenging macro environment.  Management highlighted continued market share gains, strong digital performance, and positive trends in key markets like Germany and France. However, concerns about rising inflation, consumer sentiment, and potential recessionary pressures emphasize the need for focused value initiatives and operational efficiency.  The CFO's transition signals a continuation of strategic initiatives to maintain long-term growth and resilience.
