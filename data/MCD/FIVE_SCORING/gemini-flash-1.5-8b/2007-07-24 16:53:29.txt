McDonald's earnings call highlights continued strong global performance, driven by menu innovation, particularly in breakfast and coffee, and successful promotions.  Management expressed optimism about future growth, with plans to continue expanding into new areas and optimizing operations. However, there are some concerns about the impact of cost inflation,  specifically,  labor and commodity costs, which may put pressure on margins in the short term despite good comparable sales growth.  The outlook for 2008 is positive for immediate sales but subject to further IRS audit resolution.
