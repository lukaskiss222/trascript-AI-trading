McDonald's 2019 earnings call highlights strong system-wide sales growth (100 Billion in system-wide sales and comp sales) and high owner/operator cash flow in key markets.  Management emphasizes the Velocity Growth Plan's success in various segments, particularly the US turnaround, and future focus on digital and delivery initiatives.  Short-term implications include a focus on positive guest count growth in the US, managing rising costs, and increased competitive pressure, especially in the breakfast market.
