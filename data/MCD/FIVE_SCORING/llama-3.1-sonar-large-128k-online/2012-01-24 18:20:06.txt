McDonald's Q4 and full-year 2011 earnings call-presented several positive and stable indicators despite the challenging global economic environment. Here are the key points:

1. **Strong Sales and Profitability**:

2. **Market Share and Customer Growth**:
   - The company continued to gain market share, with over 350 million additional customer visits in the U.S. compared to 2010. Global customer visits reached nearly 68 million per day.

5. **Guidance and Outlook**:
