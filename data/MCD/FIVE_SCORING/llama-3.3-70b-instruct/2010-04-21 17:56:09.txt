McDonald's Q1 earnings call revealed strong global comparable sales growth of 4.2%, with operating income increasing 13% in constant currency and EPS rising 9% in constant currency. The company's focus on menus, convenience, and re-imaging efforts drove sales, with Europe and APMEA showing significant comparable sales growth. Management expressed confidence in the company's ability to continue delivering strong results, driven by its plan to win strategy. The stock is likely to experience a positive impact in the short term due to the strong earnings results and positive guidance.
