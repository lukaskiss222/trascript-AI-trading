Management's tone was optimistic, highlighting the company's ability to balance innovation with disciplined execution and its commitment to delivering long-term sustainable growth. With a solid track record of performance and a strong foundation for future growth, McDonald's is well-positioned to continue delivering value to its customers, system, and shareholders.
