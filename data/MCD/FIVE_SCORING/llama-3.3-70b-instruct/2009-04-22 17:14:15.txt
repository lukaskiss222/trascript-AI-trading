McDonald's Corporation reported strong first-quarter results, with global comparable sales up 4.3% and operating income increasing 5% in constant currency. The company's focus on customer relevance, convenience, and value has contributed to its success, and management expects this momentum to continue. However, there are some concerns about the impact of economic weakness in certain markets, such as China and Germany. Despite these challenges, the company remains confident in its long-term growth potential and is committed to investing in its business to drive continued success.
