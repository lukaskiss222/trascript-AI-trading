McDonald's Corporation's earnings call revealed a strong performance in 2005, with positive comparable sales growth and improved margins. The company's plan to win, focusing on customers, products, place, price, and promotion, has driven results, and management is optimistic about 2006. The introduction of new chicken products, reimaging of restaurants, and continued focus on service and customer experience are expected to drive growth. While there are challenges in certain markets, such as the UK, the company is taking steps to address them, including refranchising and developmental licensing. Overall, the call suggests a positive outlook for the company, with expectations of continued growth and improved profitability.
