### Analysis

The earnings call for McDonald's Corporation revealed strong performance in Q1 2012, with comparable sales growth of 7.3% and operating income increasing by 9% in constant currencies. The company's strategic focus on optimizing the menu, modernizing the customer experience, and broadening accessibility has been effective in driving results. However, the company also faces challenges due to the economic environment, including varying degrees of consumer confidence and inflationary costs. The company's strong performance is a testament to its ability to execute its strategic plan effectively in a challenging environment.

### Conclusion

Despite facing economic headwinds, McDonald's continues to perform well, with strong sales and market share gains. The company's focus on value and customer experience, as well as its ability to execute its strategic plan, positions it well for continued growth. The stock is likely to see short-term positive momentum due to the company's strong performance and strategic focus.
