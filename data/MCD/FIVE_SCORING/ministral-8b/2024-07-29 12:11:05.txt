- **International Markets**: Performance in international markets remains a challenge, with negative comps in many regions. The company is working to improve value offerings and marketing strategies to address these issues.
- **Menu Innovation and Value Platforms**: The company is innovating its core menu and working on a broader value platform to address customer needs and preferences.
