Based on this earnings call transcript, McDonald's demonstrated strong performance and resilience despite challenging economic conditions. Key positives include:

- Global comparable sales up 5% for both Q4 and full year 2010
- 7th consecutive year of positive comps across all regions
- Strong market share gains, especially in the US
- Successful beverage expansion and value menu initiatives
- Healthy margins and cash flow
- Confident guidance for 2011 despite some cost pressures

Management appears confident in their ability to manage through commodity cost pressures through strategic pricing while maintaining traffic momentum. Their proven "Plan to Win" strategy continues to deliver results across markets.

While there are some headwinds (commodity costs, European austerity measures), the company's strong operational execution, value proposition, and global diversification position it well for continued growth.
