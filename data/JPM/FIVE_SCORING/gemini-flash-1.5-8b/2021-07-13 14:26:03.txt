JPMorgan Chase's earnings were strong, exceeding expectations in Investment Banking and Asset and Wealth Management, and showing healthy consumer spending.  Management discussed continued optimism about the US economy, but noted the need to remain vigilant due to the still-present uncertainties.  The lowered outlook for card net charge-offs and maintained NII guidance, while somewhat cautiously optimistic, reflects a combination of factors, including careful management of the balance sheet, and uncertainty around the pace of economic recovery.
