Salesforce's Q1 2017 earnings call highlighted strong financial results, exceeding expectations on revenue and deferred revenue growth.  Management emphasized innovation in the Sales Cloud, particularly with the Lightning platform, and the growing adoption of Salesforce's AI-powered tools.  The call also showcased impressive customer wins, strategic partnerships like the one with Amazon, and increased investment in international markets.  This positive sentiment and the raised revenue guidance suggest a positive short-term impact on the stock price.
