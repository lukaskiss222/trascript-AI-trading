Salesforce's Q3 2013 earnings call highlighted continued strong growth, with revenue exceeding expectations and a significant increase in deferred revenue.  Management emphasized the company's leadership in social, mobile, and cloud computing, showcasing strong performance across various product lines, especially the Sales, Service, and Marketing Clouds.  The guidance for FY2014, while hinting at potential headwinds from a macro perspective and FX volatility, remains optimistic, implying a positive short-term outlook for the stock price.
