Salesforce's Q3 2014 earnings call highlighted substantial revenue growth exceeding $1 billion for the first time in a quarter from an enterprise cloud computing company. Management, particularly Marc Benioff, expressed strong confidence in future growth, projecting over $5 billion in revenue for fiscal year 2015.  The call showcased new initiatives, such as Salesforce1 and the Superpod partnership with HP, suggesting a commitment to expanding its platform and service offerings, creating significant short-term optimism for sustained and further stock price appreciation.
