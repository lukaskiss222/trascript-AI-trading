Salesforce's Q1 results exceeded expectations, with revenue up 38% year-over-year to $695 million and non-GAAP EPS of $0.37, beating guidance. The company raised its full-year revenue guidance to $2.97-3 billion, representing 31-32% growth. The strong results were driven by continued adoption of the company's social enterprise vision, with notable customer wins and expansions, including a record-breaking 9-figure deal. Management expressed confidence in the company's ability to deliver robust top-line growth and improving profitability.
