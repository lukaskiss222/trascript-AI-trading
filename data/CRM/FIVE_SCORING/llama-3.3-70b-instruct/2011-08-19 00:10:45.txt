Salesforce's Q2 earnings call revealed strong financial performance, with revenue growing 38% year-over-year to $546 million, exceeding expectations. The company's customer base expanded to over 104,000, with a record 6,300 net new customers added in the quarter. Management raised full-year revenue guidance to $2.22-$2.23 billion, driven by robust demand for its cloud-based solutions. The company's strategic focus on the social enterprise, with products like Chatter and Radian6, is gaining traction, and its investment in hiring and product development is expected to drive further growth.
