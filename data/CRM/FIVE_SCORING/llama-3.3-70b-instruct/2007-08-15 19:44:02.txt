Salesforce's Q2 earnings report exceeded expectations, with revenue growing 49% year-over-year to $176.6 million and GAAP earnings per share of $0.03, beating guidance. The company's customer base expanded to 35,300, with a record 3,000 net new customers added during the quarter. The subscriber base grew 60% year-over-year to over 800,000, and the company saw significant traction with its Platform Edition, which was launched in March. Management's tone was positive, with CEO Marc Benioff expressing confidence in the company's competitive position and growth prospects. The company's guidance for Q3 and FY08 was also strong, with revenue expected to range from $187-189 million in Q3 and $727-732 million for the full year.
