Salesforce's Q3 earnings call revealed a strong quarter with revenue exceeding expectations, growing 57% year-over-year to $130 million, and adding 61,000 net new subscribers. The company's guidance for Q4 and fiscal year 2008 also suggests continued momentum. The introduction of Apex and the success of AppExchange, with over 430 applications, demonstrate the company's ability to innovate and expand its offerings. Management's tone was optimistic, highlighting the company's leadership in the on-demand market and its growing partnerships with major systems integrators like IBM.
