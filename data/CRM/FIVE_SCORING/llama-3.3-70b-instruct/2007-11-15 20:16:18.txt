The company's international business is accelerating, with revenue growing 92% in Asia and 71% in Europe. Management provided strong guidance for Q4 and fiscal 2009, with revenue expected to reach $1 billion. The company's multi-application, multi-category strategy is working, with a growing number of customers using its platform to build custom applications. Overall, the earnings call suggests a positive outlook for the company, with strong revenue growth and expanding margins.
