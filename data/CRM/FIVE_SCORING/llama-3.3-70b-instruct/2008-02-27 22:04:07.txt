Salesforce.com's Q4 earnings call revealed exceptional financial performance, with revenue increasing 50% year-over-year to $217 million and GAAP operating margin expanding to 4.9%. The company's customer base grew to over 41,000, with notable wins in various industries, including financial services and telecommunications. The Force.com platform is gaining traction, with over 64,000 custom applications created by customers. Management expressed confidence in the company's ability to execute and achieve its goal of $1 billion in revenue in fiscal 2009. The strong financial results and positive guidance suggest a short-term positive impact on the stock price.
