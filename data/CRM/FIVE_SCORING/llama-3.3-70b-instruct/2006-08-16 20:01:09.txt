Salesforce.com's Q2 earnings call revealed strong financial performance, with revenue up 64% year-over-year and non-GAAP EPS of $0.06, exceeding expectations. The company's customer growth was also impressive, with 57,000 net new subscribers added during the quarter, bringing the total to over 500,000. Management's tone was optimistic, with a focus on innovation and growth, particularly with the AppExchange platform. Given the strong results and positive guidance, the stock is likely to experience a positive impact in the short term.
