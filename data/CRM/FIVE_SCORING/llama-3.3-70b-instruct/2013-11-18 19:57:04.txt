Salesforce's Q3 earnings call revealed a strong quarter with revenue of $1.076 billion, up 36% year-over-year, and a raised revenue guidance for FY14 and FY15. The company's focus on customer technologies, cross-selling, and strategic acquisitions, such as ExactTarget, are driving growth. The introduction of Salesforce1, a new customer platform, is expected to further accelerate growth and provide new opportunities for developers, admins, and end-users. Overall, the company's performance and guidance suggest a positive outlook.
