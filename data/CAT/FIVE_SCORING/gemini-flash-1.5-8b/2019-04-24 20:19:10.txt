Caterpillar's Q1 2018 earnings call highlighted robust financial performance, with record profits per share and strong sales growth across various segments.  Management emphasized continued confidence in the diverse end markets and maintained their 2019 guidance.  While acknowledging some challenges like tariff impacts on manufacturing, the call signaled a cautious optimism about continued growth and order trends in the second half of the year, driven by initiatives in services, digitization, and product expansion.
