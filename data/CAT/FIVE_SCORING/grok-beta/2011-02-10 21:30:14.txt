Caterpillar Inc. reported strong financial performance for 2010, with significant sales increases and improved profitability, surpassing expectations. However, Caterpillar also highlighted concerns about production constraints, potential cost pressures, and the uncertain residential construction market in North America. Given these mixed signals—strong growth forecasts coupled with short-term operational challenges—the stock is likely to experience a positive impact in the next 1-2 weeks as investors might see this as an attractive opportunity despite the acknowledged headwinds.
