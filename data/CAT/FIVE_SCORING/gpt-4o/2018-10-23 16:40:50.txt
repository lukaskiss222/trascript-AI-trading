Caterpillar Inc. reported strong Q3 2018 performance with record profit per share for the third consecutive quarter, driven by an 18% increase in sales compared to the previous year. The company maintained its full-year adjusted profit per share guidance and expressed confidence in a continued improvement in market conditions, supported by favorable commodity prices and strong infrastructure demand. Management conveyed optimism for 2019, suggesting further growth prospects despite geopolitical risks and trade tensions. The positive tone and robust financial results suggest a short-term positive impact on the stock.
