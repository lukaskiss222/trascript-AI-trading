Honeywell's earnings call highlights strong performance in the face of a challenging economic climate.  Despite a 6% sales decline, they achieved 7% EPS growth and impressive free cash flow conversion.  Management is cautious about the immediate future, projecting a tougher first half of 2009, but optimistic about the long term, pointing to investments made in recent years to improve operational efficiency and a diversified portfolio positioning them better to navigate the downturn.
