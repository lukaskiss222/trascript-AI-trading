Honeywell's Q1 2015 earnings show strong margin expansion despite some reported sales declines.  The company pointed to cost-cutting initiatives, favorable currency hedging, and divestitures as key drivers.  While the macro environment presented challenges, particularly in oil and gas, management highlighted positive trends in other sectors, like aerospace and ACS, suggesting continued momentum in the short term and raising EPS guidance, indicating a likely positive impact on the stock.
