Honeywell's earnings call highlighted sequential improvement from Q2, with adjusted EPS up 24% and organic sales decline narrowing to 14%.  Management expressed confidence in a continued recovery, projecting roughly a 12% decrease in organic sales for Q4, with a return to margin expansion.  The focus on new growth areas like PPE and warehouse automation, alongside cost-cutting measures, suggests a positive outlook for the next 1-2 weeks, although the ongoing impact of the pandemic remains a factor.
