Honeywell's Q2 2019 earnings show strong performance across many segments, particularly in aerospace and related areas, with organic growth and margin expansion.  However, weakness in the productivity products segment and cautious guidance for the short cycle businesses in the second half of the year suggest potential short-term headwinds.  Management highlighted a focus on cost-cutting and repositioning, emphasizing their commitment to long-term growth despite near-term uncertainties in the macro environment.
