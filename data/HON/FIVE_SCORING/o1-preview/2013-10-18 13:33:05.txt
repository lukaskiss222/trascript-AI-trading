Honeywell reported strong EPS growth at the high end of guidance, raised the low end of its full-year EPS outlook, and achieved significant margin expansion. Management expressed confidence in accelerating organic growth in 2014, highlighting positive trends in key markets like the U.S., Europe, and China. Despite some sales shortfalls due to delayed acquisitions and defense sector challenges, the optimistic outlook and improved guidance suggest a positive short-term impact on the stock.
