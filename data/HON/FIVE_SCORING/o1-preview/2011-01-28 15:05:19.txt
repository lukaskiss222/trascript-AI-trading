Honeywell reported strong Q4 results with sales and earnings exceeding expectations, and raised its earnings guidance for 2011. Management expressed confidence in future growth, highlighting robust performance across all segments, significant contract wins, and strategic moves such as the sale of the CPG business with plans to reinvest proceeds into share buybacks and pension contributions. The positive performance and optimistic outlook are likely to have a positive short-term impact on the stock price.
