Honeywell International Inc.'s Q2 2020 earnings call revealed a strong start to the year, with better-than-expected performance in many end markets. The company reported sales of $8.2 billion, up 8% from the previous year, and earnings per share of $0.60, up 24% excluding pension expenses. The company's aerospace, automation and control solutions, and transportation systems segments all showed improvements, with ACS and Transportation Systems delivering strong margin expansions. Despite some economic uncertainties, Honeywell raised its full-year guidance, expecting sales in the range of $32.4 to $32.9 billion and earnings in the range of $2.40 to $2.50 per share.
