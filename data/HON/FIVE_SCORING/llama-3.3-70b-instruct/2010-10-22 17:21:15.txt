Honeywell International Inc.'s Q3 earnings call revealed a strong quarter with revenues and earnings beating expectations, driven by continued growth in emerging regions and a recovery in commercial aerospace. The company raised its full-year guidance, driven by better-than-expected performance across all segments. Despite labor cost headwinds, Honeywell's margin expansion and cash flow generation were impressive, with free cash flow conversion exceeding 200%. The company's outlook for 2011 is cautiously optimistic, with expected sales growth of around 5% and double-digit operating earnings growth.
