Honeywell's Q4 earnings call revealed a strong finish to 2007, with 12% sales growth and 26% EPS growth, driven by a diversified portfolio and successful execution of key initiatives. Despite anticipation of a softer economic environment in 2008, management expressed confidence in their ability to deliver high-teens EPS growth and 100%+ free cash flow conversion, driven by continued momentum in their businesses and a focus on cost management and productivity initiatives. The company's guidance for 2008 remains unchanged, with expected revenues of $38.2-$40.2 billion and EPS of $3.55-$3.80, indicating a positive outlook for the stock in the short term.
