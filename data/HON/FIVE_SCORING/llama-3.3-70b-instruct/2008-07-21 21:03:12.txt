Honeywell International Inc. has reported strong Q2 2008 earnings, with sales up 13% and EPS increasing 23% to $0.96, beating guidance. The company has raised its full-year EPS guidance to $3.75-$3.85, driven by solid performance across all segments, particularly aerospace and specialty materials. Management's tone is cautious but confident, highlighting the company's diversified portfolio, internal initiatives, and ability to execute in a tougher economic environment.
