Honeywell International Inc.'s Q4 earnings call revealed a strong finish to 2010, with better-than-expected results driven by higher sales and better productivity. The company's guidance for 2011 has been raised, with expected earnings per share of $3.60 to $3.80, driven by good organic growth, strong sales conversion, and continued traction on commercial and R&D effectiveness. While there are some headwinds, such as labor cost actions and potential defense budget cuts, the company is confident in its ability to deliver strong results.
