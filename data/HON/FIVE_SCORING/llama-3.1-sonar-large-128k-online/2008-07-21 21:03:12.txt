- **Repositioning and Restructuring**: Significant repositioning actions and restructuring efforts are underway to improve future performance, with over $160 million spent in the first half of 2008.
- **Positive Outlook**: Despite economic headwinds, the company remains confident in its ability to deliver double-digit earnings growth and strong free cash flow conversions.
