Honeywell International Inc. reported strong financial performance for Q4 2011, showing significant organic growth, margin expansion, and robust earnings per share surpassing expectations. The management expressed confidence in future growth, supported by strong performance in long-cycle businesses and continued global expansion, particularly in emerging markets. While they noted challenges due to economic uncertainties in Europe and some short-cycle pressures, the guidance for 2012 remains optimistic, indicating confidence in sustained growth and margin improvements.
