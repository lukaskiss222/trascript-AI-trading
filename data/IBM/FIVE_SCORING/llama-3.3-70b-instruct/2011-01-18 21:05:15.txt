IBM's Q4 earnings call revealed a strong finish to 2010, with 7% revenue growth, driven by hardware and software transactional businesses, and a 16% increase in earnings per share. The company's growth initiatives, such as Business Analytics and Smarter Planet, also showed impressive growth. Management expressed confidence in the company's momentum going into 2011, with a strong hardware lineup, backlog growth in services, and ongoing productivity initiatives. The new 2015 roadmap objective of at least $20 in operating EPS was also introduced, with management expecting 11% growth in 2011.
