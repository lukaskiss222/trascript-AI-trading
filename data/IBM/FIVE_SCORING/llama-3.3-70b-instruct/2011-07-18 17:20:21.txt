IBM's Q2 earnings call revealed strong performance, with 12% revenue growth and 18% operating EPS growth, driven by transactional businesses in hardware and software. The company's growth markets, business analytics, cloud, and Smarter Planet initiatives showed significant momentum, with growth markets revenue up 13% at constant currency. Management raised full-year 2011 operating EPS expectations to at least $13.25, driven by strong systems and software performance. Despite some concerns around margin pressure due to currency and acquisition integration, the overall outlook is positive.
