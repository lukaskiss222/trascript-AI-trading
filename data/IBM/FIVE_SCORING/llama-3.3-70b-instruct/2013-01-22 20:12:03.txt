IBM's earnings call suggests a positive outlook, with management expressing confidence in the company's ability to drive margin expansion through a mix shift to higher-value software and ongoing cost optimization efforts. The CFO highlighted the strong performance of the software business and expects this trend to continue, with a focus on high-growth areas like Business Analytics, Smarter Planet, and Cloud computing. Additionally, management expects positive revenue growth in the services segment, which should support the stock in the short term.
