IBM's Q4 earnings call revealed a strong performance, with the company achieving its 2010 EPS objective of $10-11 one year early. The company's focus on shifting to higher-value businesses, improving productivity, and investing in growth areas has positioned it well for 2020. Guidance for 2020 includes double-digit EPS growth, with an "at least" $11 EPS for the year. While the company faces some challenges, including a challenging economic environment and potential headwinds from currency fluctuations and pension expenses, its strong execution and flexible business model have enabled it to adapt to market conditions.
