- **Geographic Growth**: Strong growth in emerging markets, such as India, China, and Brazil, contributed to the company's overall performance. This growth is expected to continue, supporting long-term prospects.
- **Software and Services Momentum**: The software segment saw a 14% revenue growth, and services signings were exceptionally strong, with a 55% year-over-year increase. This momentum is expected to continue into 2007.
- **Cash Flow and Shareholder Returns**: IBM generated strong cash flow and returned a record $9.8 billion to investors through share repurchases and dividends, which is likely to be viewed positively by investors.
- **Guidance and Outlook**: Management expressed confidence in maintaining their long-term earnings growth model of 10% to 12%, and the analyst estimates for 2007 were deemed reasonable by the CFO.
