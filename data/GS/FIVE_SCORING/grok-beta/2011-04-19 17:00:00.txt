Goldman Sachs reported solid Q1 2011 results with revenues of $11.9B and earnings per share beating expectations, and while some underlying businesses showed resilience amidst uncertainty, the broader market's cautious stance suggests limited short-term impact on stocks. The cautious macro environment and regulatory concerns overshadow the company's productive client executions and strategic moves. Yet, lower leverage and investment in technology posture Goldman for potential positive long-term outcomes.
