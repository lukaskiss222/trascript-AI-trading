Goldman Sachs' Q3 2011 earnings were significantly impacted by a weak global economic environment, leading to lower client activity and significant unrealized losses in their Investing & Lending portfolio.  While Investment Banking and Institutional Client Services showed some resilience, the overall performance fell short of expectations.  Management acknowledged the challenges and expressed cautious optimism about the medium-to-long-term outlook, but the immediate short-term outlook for the stock is likely negative.
