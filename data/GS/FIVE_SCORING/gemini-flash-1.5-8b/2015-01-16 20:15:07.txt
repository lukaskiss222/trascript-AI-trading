Goldman Sachs' Q4 2014 earnings call highlighted a stable, though somewhat subdued, performance across most segments.  While Investment Banking showed strength in advisory, some underwriting areas lagged expectations.  Management stressed the firm's long-term strategy, emphasizing client relationships and adaptability to regulatory changes.  The stock price likely to be impacted minimally in the near-term; the long-term implications of the evolving regulatory landscape and overall market conditions will shape investor perception.
