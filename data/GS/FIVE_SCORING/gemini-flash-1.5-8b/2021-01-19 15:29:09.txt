Goldman Sachs' earnings call highlights strong performance despite a challenging year.  Record revenues and EPS, driven by increased client activity and market share gains, suggest a robust outlook.  Management noted the potential for continued strength in investment banking and favorable market conditions, although cautions remain about COVID-19 and global economic recovery.  The focus on new business lines (transaction banking, Marcus) and continued organic growth in core businesses signals an emphasis on long-term sustainability.
