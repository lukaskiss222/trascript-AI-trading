Goldman Sachs' Q3 2009 earnings were strong, exceeding expectations in several areas, particularly in Fixed Income, Currency, and Commodities (FICC).  Management emphasized continued strong client activity, a robust backlog in investment banking, and a focus on managing risk and capital prudently.  The positive news, coupled with stable trading volumes and a continued focus on client service, suggests a positive short-term outlook for the stock price.
