Goldman Sachs reported lower revenues and earnings for the quarter, with net revenues of $6.6 billion and net earnings of nearly $1 billion. The management highlighted macroeconomic uncertainties, particularly in Europe, leading to reduced client activity and a lower return on equity of 5.4%. Despite cost-cutting measures and share repurchases, the cautious outlook and decreased profitability may result in short-term negative pressure on the stock price.
