4. **Capital Management and Regulatory Compliance**: The firm made strides in improving its capital ratios, especially with the sale of its reinsurance business, which enhanced its Basel III compliance. Goldman Sachs is also well-positioned regarding the proposed leverage ratio[4].

5. **Cautious Optimism**: Management expressed cautious optimism about the outlook, emphasizing the importance of disciplined risk management and capital allocation. This cautious tone might temper immediate market enthusiasm[4].
