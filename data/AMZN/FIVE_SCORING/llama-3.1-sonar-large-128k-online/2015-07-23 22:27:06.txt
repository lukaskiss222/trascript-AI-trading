Based on the Q2 2015 earnings transcript for Amazon.com, Inc., here are the key points that influence the short-term impact on the stock price:

## Strong Financial Performance
- Amazon reported a 20% year-over-year revenue growth to $23.18 billion, with a significant increase in operating income and free cash flow. This strong financial performance is likely to positively impact the stock price.

## AWS Growth
- The Amazon Web Services (AWS) segment showed an 81% revenue growth, with a 407% increase in operating income. This robust growth in AWS, a high-margin business, is a positive indicator for the company's profitability and future growth.

## Operational Efficiencies
- The company highlighted various efficiency improvements, including reduced costs in fulfillment and technology, which contributed to higher operating margins. This indicates effective management and potential for sustained profitability.

## International and Prime Growth
- International revenue growth, particularly in EGM categories, was significant, and Prime membership continued to grow both in the U.S. and internationally. These are key drivers of long-term value and customer loyalty.

## Investment and Expansion
- Amazon's investments in new markets like India, expansion of AWS regions, and continued focus on innovation and customer experience are positive indicators for future growth.

Given these points, the overall sentiment from the earnings call is positive, indicating strong performance across key business segments, efficient operations, and promising growth opportunities.
