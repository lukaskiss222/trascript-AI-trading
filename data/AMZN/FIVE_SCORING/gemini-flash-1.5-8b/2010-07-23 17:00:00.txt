Amazon's Q2 2010 earnings call highlights strong revenue growth (41%) and robust free cash flow (29% growth).  Management emphasized continued investment in fulfillment capacity (13 new centers), suggesting ongoing expansion plans.  The call also showcased strong growth in e-commerce (EGM) and media (Kindle) segments despite some seasonality concerns in North America. This positive performance suggests a continuation of the company's growth trajectory, indicating a positive impact on the stock price in the next few weeks.
