Amazon's Q4 2008 earnings call highlights strong growth across key segments, particularly international, driven by increasing EGM revenue.  Management expressed confidence in the long-term trajectory, emphasizing customer-centric strategies and continued investment in selection and convenience.  However,  the call reveals cautious guidance for Q1 2009, indicating concern about the macro-economic environment and its potential impact on consumer spending and enterprise-level investments, which might briefly slow stock performance.
