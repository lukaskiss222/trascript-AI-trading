Amazon's Q2 2021 earnings call highlights strong Prime Day performance and continued growth in third-party seller revenue, advertising, and AWS.  However, management notes a moderation in Prime member spending growth and year-over-year comparisons due to easing pandemic restrictions, suggesting a return to more typical growth patterns.  The call anticipates continued year-over-year comparisons to be more challenging in the short-term, but overall growth remains solid.
