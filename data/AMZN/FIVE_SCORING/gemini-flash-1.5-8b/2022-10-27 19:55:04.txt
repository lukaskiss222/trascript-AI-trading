Amazon's Q3 earnings call revealed a mixed outlook, with strong revenue growth offset by foreign exchange headwinds and some signs of softening consumer demand, particularly in international markets.  Management emphasized cost-cutting measures and efficiency improvements across various segments, including AWS, to mitigate these concerns.  While Prime Day and Prime Video releases drove strong results, the lowered growth, cost pressures, and macroeconomic uncertainties imply some potential short-term pressure on the stock.
