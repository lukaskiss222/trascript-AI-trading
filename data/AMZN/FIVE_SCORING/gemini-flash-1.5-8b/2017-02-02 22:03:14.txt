Amazon's Q4 2016 earnings call demonstrates strong financial performance, with revenue growth guided to 17%-25% on an FX-neutral basis.  Management emphasizes continued investment in Prime benefits, fulfillment centers, digital content, and Alexa devices.  While these investments are likely to pressure short-term margins, the long-term growth potential of these initiatives is highlighted. The  stock will likely see positive momentum in the short-term as investors interpret the positive numbers and forward-looking outlook.
