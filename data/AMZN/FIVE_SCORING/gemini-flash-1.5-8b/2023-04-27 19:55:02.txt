Amazon's Q1 2023 earnings were strong, exceeding guidance on both revenue and operating income.  Management highlighted continued inflationary pressure easing, strong performance in international, and investments in cost-optimizing strategies.  The cautious spending environment noted by management is reflected in lower anticipated CapEx in 2023, particularly retail, potentially driving short-term profit margin gains, though, long-term investments in AWS and generative AI are expected to continue.
