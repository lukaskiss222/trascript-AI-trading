Amazon's Q1 2008 earnings call highlighted strong revenue growth (37%) across all segments, despite a slight decrease in gross margins due to lower prices and product mix changes.  Management expressed confidence in the long-term outlook, projecting continued growth and emphasizing the company's focus on providing excellent customer experiences across retail and third-party marketplaces.  Short-term stock implications appear positive due to the strong financial performance and supportive guidance amid uncertain economic conditions.
