Amazon’s Q1 2024 results and outlook indicate strong positive momentum, suggesting a likely positive impact on the stock in the short term. The company reported significant revenue and operating income growth, driven by advancements in its core businesses such as AWS, which saw a reacceleration in growth partly due to generative AI. Positive developments in delivery speeds, margin improvements in international markets, and strong advertising growth further bolster Amazon’s position. Guidance for Q2 is optimistic despite mentioning potential FX headwinds, pointing to continued robust performance across multiple segments.
