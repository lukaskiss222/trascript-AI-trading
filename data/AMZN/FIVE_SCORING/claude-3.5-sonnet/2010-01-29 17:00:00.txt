Amazon's Q4 2009 earnings call revealed exceptionally strong performance and positive indicators. Key highlights include:

1. Revenue growth of 42% to $9.52B, significantly beating expectations
2. Strong margin improvements in North America (211 basis points increase)
3. Healthy growth across all segments (Media, EGM, International)
4. Robust cash flow growth of 114% to $2.92B
5. Strong forward guidance suggesting continued momentum

The management's confidence in continued growth, successful integration of Zappos, and strong performance across multiple revenue streams indicates very positive short-term momentum. The company's focus on customer experience and pricing strategy while maintaining profitability suggests continued strength.
