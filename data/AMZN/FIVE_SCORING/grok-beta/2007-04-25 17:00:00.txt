Amazon's Q1 2007 results showed robust growth in both sales revenue and customer engagement, although gross margins contracted slightly and inventory turnover decreased. Concerns may arise from the potential volatility in gross margins and the conservative guidance for sequential quarters. However, the company's strong growth in electronics and media, expansion of Prime, and investment in e-commerce infrastructure suggests a positive short-term outlook for stock performance as these initiatives should drive future customer loyalty and sales.
