Amazon's Q4 2009 results showcased strong growth in sales and free cash flow, with positive developments in both media and electronics & general merchandise (EGM) sectors. Despite a conservative guidance for Q1 2010, the overall positive momentum, diversifying revenue streams, and significant improvement in gross margins suggest a positive short-term stock market reaction. The focus on customer experience and selection, along with strategic investments and acquisitions like Zappos, underline Amazon's growth trajectory.
