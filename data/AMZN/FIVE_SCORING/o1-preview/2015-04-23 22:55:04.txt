Amazon.com's Q1 2015 earnings call highlighted strong revenue growth, with sales increasing by 15% to $22.72 billion and Amazon Web Services (AWS) revenue growing 49% to $1.57 billion. Management emphasized the rapid expansion of Prime membership and continued investment in the Prime platform, including services like Prime Now and original video content. While international growth was impacted by unfavorable foreign exchange rates and increased investment in markets like India, the company's focus on innovation and long-term opportunities was evident.
