Amazon's strong Q3 performance, marked by robust revenue growth and expanding operating margins, is expected to buoy its stock in the short term. The company's active customer base, expansion into new categories, and successful Prime membership program bode well for future growth. Nonetheless, Amazon's efficient operations, increased leverage, and expanding international presence point towards sustained momentum.
