Amazon's Q1 earnings call reveals continued strong growth, with particular emphasis on the adoption of its FBA and AWS services. An impressive worldwide revenue increase of 34% and substantial gains in the North American market bode well for the company. However, heavy investments and expansion plans, including 13 new fulfillment centers, may concern some investors in the short term. The stock is likely to remain stable, with some upside potential as these investmentspay off in the longer term.
