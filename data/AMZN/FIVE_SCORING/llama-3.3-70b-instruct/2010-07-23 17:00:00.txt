Amazon's Q2 earnings call revealed strong revenue growth of 41% to $6.57 billion, driven by a 42% growth in North America and 35% growth in International. The company's operating income grew 71% to $270 million, with a gross margin of 24.5%. Amazon's guidance for Q3 anticipates revenue growth of 27-40% and operating income growth of 16% decline to 24% growth. The company is investing heavily in new Fulfillment centers, with 13 new centers planned for this year, and is seeing strong growth in its Kindle business, with a tripling of the growth rate after the price reduction. Overall, the company's focus on customer experience, selection, and convenience is driving its strong growth.
