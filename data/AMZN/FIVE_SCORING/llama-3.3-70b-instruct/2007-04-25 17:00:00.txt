Amazon's Q1 2007 earnings call revealed strong financial results, with worldwide revenue growing 32% to $3.02 billion and net income increasing 118% to $111 million. The company's guidance for Q2 and the full year 2007 also suggests continued growth. Management's tone was optimistic, citing strength in the company's third-party business, improvements in COGS, and increasing scale. While there are some concerns about margin pressure and competition, the overall outlook is positive.
