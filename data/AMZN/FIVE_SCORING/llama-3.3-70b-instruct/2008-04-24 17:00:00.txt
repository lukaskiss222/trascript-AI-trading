Amazon's Q1 2008 earnings call revealed strong revenue growth of 37%, with operating income increasing 36% year-over-year. The company's guidance for 2008 suggests continued growth, with expected net sales of $19.1 billion to $20 billion. Although management expressed caution about the economic environment, the results indicate that Amazon is well-positioned to weather potential downturns, with a focus on low prices, expanding selection, and improving customer experience.
