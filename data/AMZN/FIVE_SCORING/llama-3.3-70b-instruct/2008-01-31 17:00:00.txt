Amazon's Q4 earnings transcript reveals a strong finish to 2007, with revenue growth of 39% and operating income growth of 69%. The company's focus on customer experience, selection, and convenience has driven growth across various categories and geographies. While gross margins declined due to price reductions and changes in product mix, the company's operating leverage and investments in technology and content are expected to continue. Amazon's guidance for 2008 suggests revenue growth of 26-33% and operating income growth of 20-50%. The company's long-term prospects remain strong, driven by its strategic investments and competitive advantages.
