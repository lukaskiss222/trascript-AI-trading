Boeing's Q2 results were impacted by challenges in some development programs and a weaker-than-expected commercial Airline sector. The company missed its target margins due to a combination of delayed 787 deliveries, increased infrastructure costs, and higher period expenses. However, Boeing maintains a strong backlog and its financial position remains solid. The company reaffirmed its 2008 and 2009 EPS guidance, demonstrating confidence in its ability to withstand current economic uncertainties. [-1]
