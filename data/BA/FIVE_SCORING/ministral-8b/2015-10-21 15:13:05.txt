### Analysis:

The Boeing Company's Q3 2015 earnings call revealed a strong performance with 9% revenue growth, 10.2% core operating margins, 18% growth in core earnings per share, and $2.9 billion in operating cash flow. - **Commercial Airplanes**: Revenue increased by 10% to $17.7 billion, driven by record deliveries and strong bookings. The 787 program is expected to be cash positive in Q4 and achieve 12 per month production rate in 2016.
- **Defense, Space & Security**: Revenue grew by 6%, driven by strong performance and favorable delivery mix. Key contract awards included orders from India and Japan.
- **Financial Performance**: The company generated $2.9 billion in operating cash flow, returned $8 billion to shareholders through share repurchases and dividends, and ended the quarter with $9.9 billion in cash and marketable securities.
- **Guidance**: The company provided updated guidance for 2015, expecting revenue to be between $95 billion and $97 billion, core EPS between $7.95 and $8.15, and operating cash flow to be approximately $9.5 billion.
