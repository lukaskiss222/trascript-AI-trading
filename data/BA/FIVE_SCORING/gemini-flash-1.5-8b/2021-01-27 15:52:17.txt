Boeing's earnings call highlights significant challenges related to the 737 MAX and 787 programs, with ongoing production issues impacting deliveries and profitability.  Management acknowledges the uneven recovery in the commercial aviation market due to COVID-19, and expects a multi-year recovery process with continued pressure on cash flow stemming from production delays and customer settlements.  The company is taking steps to streamline operations and prioritize 737 and 787 deliveries to bolster cash flow and address inventory concerns.
