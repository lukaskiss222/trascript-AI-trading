Boeing's Q3 2022 earnings call highlighted positive free cash flow, a significant accomplishment after years of challenges.  However, substantial charges related to fixed-price development contracts in the defense segment tempered the overall positive outlook.  Supply chain constraints and labor shortages continue to impact production, particularly regarding engine deliveries, which will likely continue to impact deliveries into 2023.  While demand remains strong, the pace of recovery and deleveraging is slower than initially anticipated.
