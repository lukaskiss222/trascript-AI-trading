Boeing's earnings call highlights significant uncertainty due to the COVID-19 pandemic and its impact on the commercial aerospace sector.  Reduced production rates, grounded aircraft, and airline order cancellations are driving near-term challenges.  However, management emphasizes a measured approach to recovery, with a focus on preserving liquidity and adapting to market conditions.  Deliveries of finished aircraft are expected to be a key driver in the second half of 2020.
