Boeing's earnings call highlighted continued strong performance, record deliveries, and a healthy backlog across commercial and defense segments.  Management expressed confidence in long-term growth, emphasizing the value proposition of new aircraft models like the 737 MAX and 787-10.  The call also addressed the potential impacts of tax reform, indicating further investment in innovation and workforce development.  Short-term impacts may be muted as the company focuses on balancing cash deployment and strategic investments.
