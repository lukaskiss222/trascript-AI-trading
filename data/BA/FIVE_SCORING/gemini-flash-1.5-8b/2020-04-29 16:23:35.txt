Boeing's earnings call highlighted the severe impact of the COVID-19 pandemic on the aviation industry.  Reduced production rates across commercial airplane programs, coupled with customer deferrals and grounding of the 737 MAX, are expected to significantly affect short-term cash flow.  While management emphasizes government support and the strength of the defense sector,  the overall outlook for the next 1-2 weeks is negative given the uncertainty over customer demand and the planned workforce reductions.
