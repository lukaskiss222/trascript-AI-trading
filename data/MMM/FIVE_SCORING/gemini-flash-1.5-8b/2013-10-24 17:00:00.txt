3M's Q3 earnings call highlighted strong organic growth (5.8% local currency) across all business segments, exceeding expectations.  Management emphasized continued investment in innovation and transformation initiatives, primarily ERP implementation.  While guidance was narrowed to a slightly lower EPS range and management indicated some cautiousness regarding the electronics and energy segment heading into Q4, the overall tone was positive, suggesting a likely positive near-term impact on the stock price.
