3M's Q2 2020 earnings call showed a mixed picture.  While sales were down significantly year-over-year due to the pandemic's impact on various end markets, the company delivered strong operational performance with cost savings and increased adjusted free cash flow.  Management highlighted sequential improvement in sales trends in July and projected continued growth, particularly in respirators and certain consumer segments, suggesting a potential recovery.  However, uncertainty about the pace of economic recovery and fluctuating demand remain.
