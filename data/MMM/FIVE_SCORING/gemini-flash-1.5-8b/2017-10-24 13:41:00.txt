3M's Q3 2017 earnings call highlighted strong, broad-based organic growth across all business segments and geographic areas.  Management expressed confidence in the sustainability of this growth, particularly in Electronics & Energy and developing markets.  While some concerns were raised around potentially soft spots in the office and stationery markets, the overall tone was positive, with management reiterating commitment to research and development and acquisitions in strategic areas like safety.  This suggests a positive short-term outlook for the stock.
