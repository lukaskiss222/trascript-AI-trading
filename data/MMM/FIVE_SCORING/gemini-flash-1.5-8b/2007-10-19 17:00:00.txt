3M's Q3 2007 earnings call highlighted robust growth in healthcare, international markets, and certain industrial segments.  Management emphasized ongoing investments in R&D, acquisitions, and international expansion, while acknowledging some headwinds in the US housing market and consumer electronics.  Given the maintained confidence in the long-term strategy and continued investments, the short-term impact on the stock is likely to be positive, albeit potentially tempered by ongoing price pressures in certain sectors.
