3M's Q4 earnings call revealed a strong quarter with sales growth of 11.1% and operating margins of 21.9%. The company's health care and consumer businesses drove growth, with health care sales up 11% and consumer sales up 11.4%. 3M also saw growth in its industrial and transportation segment, with sales up 10%. The company's guidance for 2010 is positive, with expected EPS of $4.90 to $5.10, representing an 8-13% increase over 2009. Management's tone was optimistic, citing the company's ability to navigate the economic downturn and its confidence in its future growth prospects.
