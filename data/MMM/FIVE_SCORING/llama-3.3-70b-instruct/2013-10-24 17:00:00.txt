3M's Q3 earnings call revealed a strong quarter with record sales and broad-based growth across all business segments. The company posted an organic local currency growth of 5.8%, led by the Safety and Graphics segment at 8%. While management expressed caution regarding the consumer electronics market, they remain optimistic about the company's long-term prospects. With a narrowed guidance range for 2013 and expectations of continued growth, 3M's stock is likely to experience a positive impact in the short term.
