### Analysis:

The management team acknowledged these challenges and announced several aggressive cost-cutting measures, including reducing approximately 2,000 positions, to improve productivity and reduce costs.

The company also provided updated guidance for the full year, lowering the range for organic growth to minus one to plus 2% and adjusted EPS to $9.25 to $9.75 per share. This guidance reflects the company's expectation of a slower economic environment and the impact of restructuring efforts.

The management team emphasized their commitment to strategic growth and diversification, highlighting the importance of the priority growth platforms and the potential for acquisitions. They also addressed the ongoing litigation related to PFAS and respirators, which has been a significant headwind for the company. The management team stated that they are actively managing the litigation and working with regulatory bodies to support science-based regulations.

The short-term impact on the stock price is likely to be negative, given the disappointing first-quarter results and the lowered guidance for the full year.
