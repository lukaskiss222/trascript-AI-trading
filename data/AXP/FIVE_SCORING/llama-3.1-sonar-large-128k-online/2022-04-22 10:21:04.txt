2. **Customer Acquisition and Engagement**: The company saw record acquisitions of premium cards, particularly among millennial and Gen Z consumers, and small and medium-sized businesses. Customer engagement metrics, such as spending growth and use of digital capabilities, also showed significant improvement[4].

4. **Credit Performance**: American Express reported strong credit performance, with delinquencies and net write-offs remaining below pre-pandemic levels. The company also benefited from a release of pandemic-driven reserves, contributing to a positive provision expense[4].

5. **Strategic Initiatives and Partnerships**: The company is expanding its value propositions through new partnerships, such as with Vanguard and i2c, and enhancing its services to attract and retain customers. These initiatives are expected to drive further growth and engagement[4].
