American Express reported solid Q1 2012 results with an 8% revenue increase and a notable 10% EPS growth, driven by strong billed business growth and stable credit metrics. The company successfully managed expenses, holding them nearly flat after adjustments, while continuing share buybacks and dividend increases. Although the challenging European economic environment and regulatory uncertainties persist, the company maintained its growth momentum and operational performance, suggesting a positive short-term outlook for the stock.
