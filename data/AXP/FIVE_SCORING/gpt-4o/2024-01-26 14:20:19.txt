American Express reported strong financial results for Q4 2023, including record revenues and solid EPS figures. The company highlighted growth in premium products and a large influx of new card accounts, driven primarily by Millennials and Gen Z customers, while maintaining strong credit metrics. Although there is a slight slowdown in some segments such as travel and entertainment, AXP remains confident in its long-term growth trajectory, reflecting a positive tone from management. This, along with increased dividends and favorable guidance for 2024, suggests a potential positive impact on the stock price in the short term.
