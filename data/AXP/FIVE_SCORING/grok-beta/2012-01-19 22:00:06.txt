American Express achieved strong financial performance in Q4 2011, with record billed business and EPS growth, but missed their typical 8% revenue growth target. Despite this, their focus on premium lending, growth in card bases, and investment in new business initiatives, along with the management's positive outlook on future business growth, suggest a short-term positive impact on stock price. However, the tougher year-over-year comparisons and uncertainties in global economic conditions could temper this positivity slightly.
