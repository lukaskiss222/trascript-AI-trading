American Express's Q3 2020 earnings call highlights a continued recovery in spending, particularly in non-travel categories.  Management expresses cautious optimism, positioning the company for a second-phase of investment and growth based on improving trends and growing confidence in the longer-term recovery. The focus on retaining customers and accelerating strategic investments in key areas (like acquiring small businesses via Kabbage) suggests they expect a positive impact on the stock price over the next 1-2 weeks.
