American Express' Q1 2015 earnings, while showing positive EPS growth (11%), were impacted by FX headwinds and the termination of the Costco Canada cobrand relationship.  Management emphasized solid core performance and maintained their full-year outlook despite these discrete factors.  The expected slowdown in corporate spending, particularly in the US, and the impact of the Costco Canada closure could potentially affect future quarters' performance, so analysts should expect a more uneven earnings pattern.
