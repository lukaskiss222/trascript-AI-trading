American Express's Q3 2012 earnings call highlighted a slight slowdown in revenue growth compared to previous quarters, but management emphasized continued strength in the face of economic uncertainty.  The call highlighted stable credit metrics, but increased provision expenses due to lower reserve releases.  Management's proactive approach to managing expenses, a focus on premium lending, and organic growth strategies, including the rebranding of Serve, suggest a plan for navigating potential economic headwinds.
