American Express' Q3 2021 earnings call highlighted strong growth in goods and services spending, driven by resilient SME and Millennial/Gen Z customer segments.  Management expressed confidence in continued investment in marketing and product innovation to sustain long-term growth, potentially exceeding original 2020 EPS targets.  The call suggests a positive short-term outlook despite the lag in travel and entertainment recovery, particularly with the continued strength in online spending.
