The American Express Q1 2023 earnings call revealed strong financial performance with record revenues and earnings per share. The company's growth was driven by robust spending across all segments, particularly in travel and entertainment (T&E) and premium fee-based products. The company's capital position and liquidity are strong, allowing it to maintain a healthy balance sheet and continue share buybacks.
