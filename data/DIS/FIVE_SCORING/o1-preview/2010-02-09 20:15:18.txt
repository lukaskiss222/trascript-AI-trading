Walt Disney Company reported better-than-expected Q1 results, with strong performances from its media networks, particularly ESPN, and higher attendance at Parks and Resorts. Management expressed optimism about upcoming releases like "Toy Story 3" and "Iron Man 2," and highlighted cost-saving measures and strategic investments in new technologies like the iPad. This positive financial performance and upbeat outlook are likely to boost investor confidence and have a positive short-term impact on the stock price.
