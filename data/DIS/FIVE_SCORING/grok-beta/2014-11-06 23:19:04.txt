The Walt Disney Company's announcement of their 2014 fiscal year results was very positive, with a 21% increase in operating income and a robust content pipeline, including high-visibility franchises like Marvel, Star Wars, and Pixar. This suggests potential for strong revenue growth due to the cultural impact and expected market performance of these franchises. However, the discussion on changes in the media landscape and the sustainability of the multi-channel subscription model injects some uncertainty.
