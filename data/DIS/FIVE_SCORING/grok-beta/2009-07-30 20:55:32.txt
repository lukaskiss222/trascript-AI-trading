Disney's third quarter 2009 earnings call highlighted a challenging economic environment but showed resilience in key areas like Disney Channel growth, ESPN's stable performance, and positive feedback on upcoming films like Pixar's "UP" and the ABC fall line-up. However, the lowered home video sales, advertising market softness, and aggressive promotions in the parks suggest potential short-term pressures on stock value due to these revenue declines and cost management focus.
