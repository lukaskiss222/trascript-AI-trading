2. **Cable Networks**: ESPN continued to perform well, with double-digit increases in revenue and operating income, driven by higher subscriber fees and advertising revenue. The Disney Channel also benefited from subscriber growth in key international markets.

3. **Consumer Products**: The company experienced strong growth in licensing royalties but faced a decline in home video game sales. The acquisition of Disney Stores North America also impacted segment margins.

4. **Future Outlook**: Management was optimistic about the company's ability to manage through the challenging economic environment, emphasizing its strong brand, creative assets, and diversified franchise portfolio.
