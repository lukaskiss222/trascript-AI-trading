Disney's Q4 earnings call revealed strong financial results, with record revenue, net income, and earnings per share for the fiscal year. The company's Media Networks and Parks and Resorts segments drove the growth, with a 30% increase in net income and a 7% increase in revenue. Management's tone was optimistic, highlighting the company's strategic priorities, including creating high-quality content, expanding platforms, and growing iconic brands. The guidance for fiscal 2012 was positive, with projected sales of $715M-$836M and EPS of $4.55-$5.05. Overall, the call suggested a positive outlook for the company, with a focus on long-term growth and investment in core businesses.
