The Walt Disney Company's Q2 earnings call revealed strong financial performance, with operating income up 10% and EPS up 18% year-over-year. The company's Media Networks segment drove growth, with ESPN and Disney Channels delivering higher affiliate and advertising revenues. The Parks and Resorts segment also saw increased attendance and guest spending, with several new investments and expansions underway. Management expressed optimism about future growth prospects, citing the success of recent films like The Avengers and the potential for Marvel characters to drive consumer products sales. Overall, the call suggested a positive outlook for Disney's businesses.
