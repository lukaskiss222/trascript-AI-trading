The Walt Disney Company's earnings call revealed a strong performance in fiscal 2014, with record operating income and adjusted EPS. The company's studio business had its best year ever, driven by the success of films like Frozen, Guardians of the Galaxy, and Maleficent. Disney's media networks, including ESPN and ABC, also performed well, with ESPN having a strong upfront and ABC seeing success with its new shows. The company's parks and resorts business saw growth, driven by increases in attendance and spending at domestic parks. Looking ahead, Disney expects to continue investing in high-quality content and technology, and is well-positioned for growth in the evolving media landscape.
