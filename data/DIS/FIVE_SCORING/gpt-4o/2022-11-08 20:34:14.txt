Disney's Q4 2022 earnings call highlighted strong growth in both its Parks and the Direct-to-Consumer segment, particularly with Disney+ adding substantial new subscribers. The company outlined expectations for decreasing streaming losses and the introduction of an ad-supported tier, aiming for Disney+ profitability by 2024. Management conveyed confidence in leveraging pricing power without strongly impacting churn and emphasized strategic cost management to boost profitability. Despite some macroeconomic caution and linear network challenges, the upbeat performance in Parks and robust DTC subscriber growth suggest positive near-term impact on the stock.
