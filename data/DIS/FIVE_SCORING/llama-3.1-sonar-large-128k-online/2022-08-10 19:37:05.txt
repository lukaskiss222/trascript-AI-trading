### Negative Factors

- **Cost Pressures**: Increased programming and production costs in the direct-to-consumer segment are ongoing, which could continue to impact profitability[1].
- **Forward-Looking Concerns**: There are concerns about the potential softening of demand in Q4, which management did not address directly, leaving some uncertainty[3].

### Conclusion
Given the mixed results, with both strong revenue and profitability gains but also signs of softening demand and ongoing cost pressures, the stock is likely to experience a neutral to slightly negative short-term impact.
