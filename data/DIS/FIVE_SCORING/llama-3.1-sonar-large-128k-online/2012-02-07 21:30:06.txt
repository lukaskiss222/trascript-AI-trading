4. **Parks and Resorts Growth**: Higher guest spending and attendance at domestic parks, along with increased passenger cruise days, drove growth in Parks and Resorts. The upcoming launch of Cars Land and the Disney Fantasy cruise ship are expected to continue this trend[3%.
