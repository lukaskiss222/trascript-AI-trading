Disney's Q2 2018 earnings call highlighted significant strength in Parks & Resorts and Studio Entertainment, driven by blockbuster films like *Black Panther* and *Avengers: Infinity War*.  Management expressed confidence in the long-term growth prospects of Disney+, and positive market sentiment regarding ESPN+ bodes well.  The potential for further expansion in China and potentially globally, alongside a strategic reorganization, underscores a long-term positive outlook in the coming weeks to months.
