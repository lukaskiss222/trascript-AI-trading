Disney's Q1 FY16 earnings call highlighted phenomenal results, driven primarily by the success of *Star Wars: The Force Awakens*.  Management expressed confidence in continued growth, pointing to a strong pipeline of films, theme park expansions (especially Shanghai), and consumer product opportunities.  The call also underscored the company's strategy to leverage its brands across multiple platforms, indicating a likely positive short-term impact on the stock price as investors react to these strong results.
