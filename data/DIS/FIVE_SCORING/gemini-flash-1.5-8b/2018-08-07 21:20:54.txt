Disney's earnings call emphasized the strategy behind their significant 21st Century Fox acquisition, highlighting potential for growth in direct-to-consumer services.  Management detailed plans for a tiered approach to different types of consumers through separate apps encompassing their various content libraries (ESPN, Disney Play, Hulu).  The anticipation of a strong 2019 movie slate, free from pre-existing licensing agreements, is expected to fuel the launch of their Disney-branded streaming service, possibly driving positive impact.
