Disney's earnings call highlights strong subscriber growth for Disney+, driven by a robust content slate, especially in the second half of fiscal 2022.  Management expresses confidence in achieving profitability by fiscal 2024 and exceeding their 2024 subscriber target.  The call also notes strong attendance and spending at the parks, with Genie+ proving particularly successful.  This overall positive outlook for both streaming and theme parks suggests a positive short-term impact on the stock price.
