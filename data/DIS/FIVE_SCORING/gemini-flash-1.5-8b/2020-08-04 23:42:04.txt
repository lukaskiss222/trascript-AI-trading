Disney's Q3 2020 earnings call highlights the significant impact of the pandemic.  While the company's direct-to-consumer business, particularly Disney+, exceeded expectations, the Parks, Experiences, and Products segment faced substantial losses due to park closures.  Management's cautious optimism for the future, coupled with the aggressive push into direct-to-consumer expansion, implies a potential short-term focus on profitability via new content releases and revenue from PVOD transactions, rather than massive subscriber growth.
