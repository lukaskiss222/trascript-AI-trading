Visa reported strong second-quarter results with net revenues up 11% year-over-year and non-GAAP EPS increasing by 17%. Management highlighted robust growth in consumer payments, new flows, and value-added services, alongside strategic expansions and significant client renewals. Despite some short-term headwinds like moderated U.S. domestic volume growth, the overall positive performance and optimistic outlook are likely to positively influence the stock in the near term.
