### Analysis

Visa Inc. reported a strong fiscal first quarter 2021, with net revenue down 6% year-over-year and EPS down 3%. The company highlighted robust growth in debit and e-commerce spending, offset by weaker credit and in-store spending. Cross-border volume declined 33% year-over-year, but improved 8 points from the previous quarter. The company expects cross-border volume to improve further as borders reopen and travel restrictions ease. Management remains optimistic about the future, with plans to invest in growth initiatives and return capital to shareholders.
