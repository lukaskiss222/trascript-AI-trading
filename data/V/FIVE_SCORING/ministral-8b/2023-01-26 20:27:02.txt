### Analysis:
The earnings call for Visa Inc. revealed strong Q1 performance, driven by stable domestic volumes and a continued recovery in cross-border travel. Key highlights included a 12% year-over-year increase in net revenues, 21% growth in non-GAAP EPS, and robust growth across consumer payments, new flows, and value-added services. Management provided guidance for the second quarter, expecting mid-teens constant dollar net revenue growth, with a high single-digit nominal dollar growth rate. The company also discussed strategic growth initiatives, including new partnerships, expansions, and a focus on emerging markets. The transition of Al Kelly to Executive Chairman and Ryan McInerney becoming the CEO was also announced.

### Conclusion:
The stock is likely to experience a positive impact due to the strong earnings report, positive guidance, and strategic growth initiatives. The management's optimism and the company's strong financial performance suggest a positive outlook for the stock in the short term.
