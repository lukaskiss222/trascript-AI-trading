Visa Inc.'s fiscal third quarter 2023 earnings call highlighted several positive and stable trends that are likely to have a favorable short-term impact on the stock price. Here are the key points:

- **Resilient Consumer Spending**: Despite moderating inflation, consumer spending remained stable, with global payments volume up 9% and cross-border travel volumes recovering strongly[1][3][5].
- **Growth in New Flows and Value-Added Services**: New flows revenue grew 20% and value-added services revenue grew 19% in constant dollars, indicating significant opportunities in these areas[1][3].
- **Positive Outlook**: The company expressed confidence in sustaining growth trends into the fourth quarter, with expectations of around 10% net revenue growth and mid-teens EPS growth for fiscal year 2023[1][3].
- **Strategic Moves**: Visa's acquisition of Pismo and other initiatives to expand its technological and service capabilities are seen as positive steps for long-term growth[1][3].

Given these points, the stock is likely to see a positive impact in the short term due to the company's strong financial performance, resilient consumer spending, and optimistic outlook.
