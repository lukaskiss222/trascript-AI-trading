Visa's earnings call highlighted strong Q1 2022 results, driven by a robust recovery in cross-border payments, especially travel, and continued strength in domestic volumes.  Management expressed confidence in the long-term growth trajectory, emphasizing the acceleration of digitization, new payment methods like BNPL and crypto, and the expanding adoption of Visa's services.  The increased guidance and positive outlook point to a potentially significant positive impact on the stock price over the next 1-2 weeks.
