Visa's earnings call highlights the significant and varied impact of COVID-19 on their business, especially in travel-related and low-ticket transactions.  While management emphasizes the resilience of the business model and long-term growth potential, a sharply reduced outlook for the near-term, especially in cross-border and U.S. travel, is evident. This, coupled with the unknown duration and shape of the recovery, makes precise short-term predictions difficult.
