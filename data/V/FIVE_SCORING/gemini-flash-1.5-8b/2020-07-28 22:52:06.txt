Visa's Q3 2020 earnings call highlights significant declines in cross-border transaction volume, primarily due to travel restrictions.  However, domestic payments volume, particularly debit and e-commerce, showed resilience and even growth. Management expressed confidence in the long-term strategy, emphasizing the acceleration of digital payments trends like tap-and-pay and e-commerce, despite short-term headwinds.  They also emphasized the need for cautious optimism about the future of cross-border travel.
