Visa's Q1 2018 earnings call highlighted robust global payment volume growth, driven by strong performance in developed markets, particularly the US and Europe.  Management emphasized the positive impact of tax reform on earnings and announced a dividend increase and substantial share repurchase program.  While acknowledging some deceleration in cross-border volume, the overall tone was optimistic, pointing towards continued long-term growth prospects and investments in key areas like digital payments and employee benefits.
