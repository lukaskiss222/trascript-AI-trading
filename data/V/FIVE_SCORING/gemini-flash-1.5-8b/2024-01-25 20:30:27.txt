Visa's Q1 2024 earnings call highlighted robust payment volume growth, fueled by resilience in consumer spending and strong cross-border activity, especially in travel. Management's tone was optimistic, emphasizing strategic partnerships and value-added services, which are driving revenue growth.  Notably, despite some weather-related softness in January, the outlook for the remainder of the year remains positive, reinforcing expectations for continued strong growth in payments volume.
