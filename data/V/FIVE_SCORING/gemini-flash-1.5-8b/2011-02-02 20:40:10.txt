Visa's earnings call highlights strong Q1 2011 performance, with double-digit growth in payment volume, cross-border volume, and transactions. Management emphasized ongoing innovation, particularly in e-commerce and money transfer.  A significant focus is on mobile payments with the launch of contactless payments and the Visa Mobile app, supporting a positive outlook for future growth.  The company's commitment to returning excess cash to shareholders through buybacks also suggests a confident outlook.
