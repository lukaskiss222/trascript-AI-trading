Visa's Q1 2021 earnings call highlights strong performance in debit and e-commerce, exceeding expectations in some areas.  Management notes a continued focus on digitalization and new payment flows, including Visa Direct.  While cross-border volume remains depressed due to travel restrictions, the call projects a recovery in the second quarter. Overall, the tone suggests continued investment in growth areas, and the company anticipates a return to pre-pandemic growth trajectories as restrictions ease.
