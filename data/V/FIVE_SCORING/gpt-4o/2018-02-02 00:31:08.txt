Visa reported a solid fiscal Q1 2018 with a 9% net revenue growth and a 26% increase in adjusted EPS, supported significantly by U.S. tax reforms. The company continues to invest in growth areas such as digital payments, cross-border volumes, and expansions in Europe. Notably, management expressed confidence in the long-term growth potential, underscoring Visa's strategic initiatives in B2B and contactless payment solutions. The positive tone of management and strong financial performance suggest a favorable short-term outlook for Visa's stock.
