Visa reported strong Q2 2012 financial performance, with revenue and adjusted EPS showing substantial year-over-year growth. The company increased its EPS guidance for fiscal 2012, reflecting confidence in continued global payment volume growth and effective management of regulatory challenges in the U.S. debit market. Despite regulatory pressures, Visa's robust credit performance and strategic expansions, including significant international growth and technological innovations like NFC and V.me, suggest a positive outlook for the short-term. The upbeat guidance and strong fundamentals are likely to positively impact the stock in the upcoming weeks.
