Visa Inc. reported strong financial results, with net operating revenues up 15% and adjusted EPS increasing by 30%. The company raised its fiscal 2012 EPS growth guidance to high teens to low 20s, reflecting confidence in continued growth. Despite challenges in U.S. debit due to regulatory changes, Visa's strength in global credit, international expansion, and innovations in digital payments position it well for positive short-term stock performance.
