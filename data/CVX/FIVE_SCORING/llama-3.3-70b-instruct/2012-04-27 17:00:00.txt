Chevron's cash flow was also robust, with $8.4 billion generated from operations. The company's debt ratio is relatively low, at 6.9%, and it has a significant cash balance of nearly $20 billion. Chevron's management expressed confidence in the company's future cash flows and announced an 11% increase in the quarterly dividend. Overall, Chevron's results suggest a positive outlook for the company.
