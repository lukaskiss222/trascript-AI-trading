Chevron's Q3 2016 earnings call highlighted continued focus on cost reduction and capital efficiency, particularly in the Permian Basin.  Management emphasized strong performance in the Permian, with lower capital expenditures and improved operating expenses.  Guidance for production and capital expenditures in 2017-2018 aligns with continued cost-cutting, but there's a cautious outlook about sustained increases in production given the ongoing lower-for-longer price environment.
