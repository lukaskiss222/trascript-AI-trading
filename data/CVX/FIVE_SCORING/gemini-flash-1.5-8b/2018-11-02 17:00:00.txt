Chevron's Q3 2018 earnings call highlighted strong financial performance, driven by high oil prices and robust production, particularly in the Permian.  Management expressed confidence in achieving their 2018 guidance, while acknowledging potential challenges in the downstream segment due to planned turnarounds.  The call emphasized continued focus on portfolio optimization and shareholder returns, potentially leading to further share repurchases, given the current strong cash flow from operations.
