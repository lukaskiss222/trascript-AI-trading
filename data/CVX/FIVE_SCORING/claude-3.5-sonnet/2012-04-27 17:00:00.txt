Based on this detailed earnings call transcript, I anticipate a positive short-term impact on Chevron's stock price for the following key reasons:

1. Strong financial performance with Q1 earnings of $6.5B ($3.27 per share), up 4% year-over-year, driven by higher crude prices and downstream asset sales.

- Higher operating costs, though largely tied to compensation for strong 2011 performance

Overall, the strong earnings, cash generation, project execution and shareholder returns should drive positive sentiment in the near-term.
