3. **Dividends and Share Repurchases**: Chevron declared a $1.07 per share quarterly common dividend and repurchased $1.25 billion of shares in the first quarter.

4. **Guidance**: The company provided updated guidance for the year, with expectations for overall production increases driven by growth in shale and tight resources in the Permian and Argentina.

5. **Major Projects**: Progress was made on major projects such as Jack/St. Malo, Tubular Bells, and Big Foot. The LNG projects Gorgon and Wheatstone are on track for startup in mid-2015 and late 2016, respectively.

6. **Shale and Tight Resources**: Chevron is actively drilling and developing in the Permian Basin and Vaca Muerta Shale in Argentina, with a focus on capital efficiency and execution.
