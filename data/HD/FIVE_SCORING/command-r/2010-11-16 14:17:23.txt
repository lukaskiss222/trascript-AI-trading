Following a stabilisation period, The Home Depot's third-quarter earnings call revealed positive indicators. Rising transactions and a narrowing gap between top and underperforming stores demonstrate broad-based improvement. Management's focus on technology to enhance customer experience and operational efficiency is promising. However, big-ticket item sales remain under pressure due to the housing market. With a positive outlook for the holiday season and continued expense leverage, the short-term impact on the stock is likely positive.
