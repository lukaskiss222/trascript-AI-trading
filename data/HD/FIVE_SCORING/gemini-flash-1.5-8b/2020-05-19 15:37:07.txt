The Home Depot's Q1 2020 earnings call highlighted strong online sales growth and positive comp sales, largely driven by increased DIY demand.  However, management expressed caution about extrapolating trends due to the unpredictable nature of the COVID-19 pandemic's impact on the economy and customer behavior.  While guidance was suspended, the company's strong financial position and focus on associate safety and customer service suggest a potentially resilient outlook for the short term.
