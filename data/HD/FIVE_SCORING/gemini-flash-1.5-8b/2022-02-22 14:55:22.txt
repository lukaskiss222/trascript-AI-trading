The Home Depot's earnings call highlights strong Q4 performance with double-digit comparable sales growth driven by robust demand in home improvement.  Management emphasizes a focus on cost-pass-throughs and the company's unique position in a burgeoning market, projecting slightly positive sales growth for fiscal 2022.  The call's key takeaway for short-term implications, however, is one of continued uncertainty around inflationary pressures and supply chain stability.
