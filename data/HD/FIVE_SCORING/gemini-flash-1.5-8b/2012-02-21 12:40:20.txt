Home Depot's Q4 2011 earnings call highlighted strong sales growth, driven by positive comps in most key markets, particularly Florida.  Favorable weather significantly boosted sales in exterior categories.  Management anticipates continued growth in line with US GDP in 2012, though acknowledging uncertainties in the housing market.  The call suggests potential for continued stock price appreciation over the next 1-2 weeks due to the positive sales figures and confident guidance.
