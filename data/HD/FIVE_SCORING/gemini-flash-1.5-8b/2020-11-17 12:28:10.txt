The Home Depot's Q3 2020 earnings call highlighted robust sales growth driven by strong demand for home improvement projects.  Management noted a continuation of outsized demand, with both DIY and pro customers contributing to the significant increase, and a focus on improving supply chain and customer fulfillment capabilities.  While challenges remain in the supply chain and higher lumber costs pressured margins,  the acquisition of HD Supply  and a focus on permanent wage investments signal long-term strategic investments and commitment to associates.
