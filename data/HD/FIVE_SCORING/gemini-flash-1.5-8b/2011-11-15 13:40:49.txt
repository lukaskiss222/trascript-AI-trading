Home Depot's Q3 2011 earnings call highlighted robust sales growth exceeding market expectations, driven by strong core category performance and a positive response to storm recovery efforts.  Management expressed confidence in their operational efficiency improvements and projected continued positive comp growth, despite tough comparisons in the upcoming fourth quarter.  Positive guidance, coupled with share buybacks and dividend increases, suggests a bullish outlook, which should positively impact the stock in the coming weeks.
