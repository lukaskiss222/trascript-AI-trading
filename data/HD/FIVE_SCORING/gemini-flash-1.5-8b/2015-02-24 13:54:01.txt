Home Depot's Q4 2014 earnings transcript indicates strong sales growth, driven by broad-based positive comp sales and record transaction and ticket highs. Management highlights significant increases in pro-driven businesses and service offerings.  The positive guidance for 2015, projecting 3.3-4.5% comp sales growth and $5.11-$5.17 EPS, suggests continued strength in the retail landscape. Continued focus on the pro customer, online business growth, and store experience enhancements suggest a healthy outlook for the next couple of weeks.
