The Home Depot's Q2 2020 earnings call highlights strong sales growth driven by increased customer engagement in home improvement projects, driven by both DIY and Pro customers.  Management attributes this to increased time spent at home and the need for home improvements due to heightened use and more projects.  The company's interconnected retail strategy and digital capabilities facilitated record online sales and a surge in online order pickups, creating opportunities for further growth in the future.
