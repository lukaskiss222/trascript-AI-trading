The Home Depot's Q4 earnings call revealed a strong performance with record sales and earnings. The company's growth was driven by continued housing recovery, strong merchandising events, and favorable weather conditions. The company's guidance for 2016 projects sales growth of 6% and EPS growth of 13%, but currency headwinds could impact these projections.
