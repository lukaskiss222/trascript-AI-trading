The Pro business and services business also showed growth, with the services business experiencing 7 consecutive quarters of growth. The company's international businesses, particularly in Canada and Mexico, continued to perform well. However, the company faced challenges in certain categories, such as garden and building materials, due to tough year-over-year comparisons and weather conditions.

Management provided guidance for the third quarter and the full year, projecting sales growth of approximately 4.6% on a 53-week basis and diluted earnings per share of $2.95. They also mentioned the company's focus on strategic initiatives, including improving the customer experience and expanding the breadth of SKUs available online. The company's balance sheet remains strong, with a cash balance of $2.8 billion and a return on invested capital of 16%.

Overall, the results were mixed, with some categories showing strong performance while others faced challenges.
