- **Strong Comp Sales**: The company reported positive same-store sales across all U.S. divisions and regions, with U.S. stores posting a 6.3% comp sales increase in the fourth quarter. International operations also showed positive comps[1][3][4].
- **Robust Online Growth**: Online sales grew over 19% in the year, and the company's interconnected retail strategy, including initiatives like Buy Online, Deliver From Store (BODFS), has been well-received by customers[1][3][4].
- **Guidance and Capital Allocation**: The company provided strong guidance for fiscal 2017, including a 4.6% sales growth and a 10.5% increase in diluted earnings per share. Additionally, a 29% increase in the quarterly dividend and a new $15 billion share repurchase authorization were announced[1][3][4].
- **Operational Efficiencies**: The company highlighted ongoing improvements in supply chain efficiency, freight handling, and store operations, which are expected to drive continued productivity and margin expansion[1][3][4].
