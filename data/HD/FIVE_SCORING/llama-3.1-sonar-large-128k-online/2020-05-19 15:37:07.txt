This growth is positive but may not fully offset the impact of reduced store traffic and other pandemic-related disruptions[5].

4. **Geographic and Category Variability**: Sales performance varied by region and category, with some areas and categories experiencing negative impacts due to shelter-in-place orders and social distancing measures.
