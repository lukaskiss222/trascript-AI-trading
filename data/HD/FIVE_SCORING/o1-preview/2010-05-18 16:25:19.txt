Home Depot reported strong first-quarter results, with sales and comps exceeding expectations, marking the first positive U.S. comps since 2005. Management raised guidance for fiscal 2010, projecting higher sales and earnings, and emphasized continued cost control and share repurchases. Despite caution regarding pro customer growth and macroeconomic indicators, the overall positive performance and optimistic outlook are likely to positively impact the stock price in the short term.
