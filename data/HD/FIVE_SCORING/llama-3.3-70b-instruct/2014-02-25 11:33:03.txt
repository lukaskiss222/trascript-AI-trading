The Home Depot's Q4 earnings call revealed a strong performance, with sales of $17.7 billion and diluted earnings per share of $0.73, despite a challenging weather environment. The company's U.S. stores had a positive comp of 4.9%, with 17 out of 19 regions posting positive comps. The company also saw growth in its online channel, with a 50% increase in sales. Management provided guidance for 2014, expecting sales growth of 4.8% and diluted earnings per share of $4.38, representing a 16.5% increase. The company's focus on improving its online capabilities, expanding its product offerings, and enhancing the customer experience positions it well for future growth.
