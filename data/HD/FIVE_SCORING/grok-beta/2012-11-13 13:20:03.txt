The Home Depot's third quarter performance was robust with strong sales growth and positive comps at all three U.S. divisions despite tough weather comparisons. The broader U.S. housing market is showing signs of recovery, which should generally support a continuing upward trend for the stock. However, they've tempered their guidance due to uncertainties around Hurricane Sandy's impact, which could suppress short-term positive momentum. New pilots in major appliance categories and improvements in inventory management and shrink initiatives suggest efforts to drive future growth, yet the immediate term remains uncertain.
