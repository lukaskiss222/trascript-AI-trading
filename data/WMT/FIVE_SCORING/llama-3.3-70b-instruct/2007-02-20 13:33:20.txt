Walmart's Q4 and full-year earnings call revealed strong sales and earnings growth, with the company achieving record sales and earnings. The international segment drove sales momentum, with a 29.6% increase in sales, and operating income growth of 32%. The company also saw strong performances in Mexico, Brazil, and China, and made progress in its sustainability and diversity initiatives. Guidance for 2008 projects EPS of $3.15-$3.23, with U.S. comp store sales expected to increase 1%-3% in the first quarter. Overall, Walmart's results and guidance suggest a positive outlook for the company.
