Walmart's Q2 earnings call revealed a strong performance, with net sales increasing 10.4% to $101.6 billion and diluted earnings per share rising 14.7% to $0.86. The company's inventory management and merchandising efforts drove this growth, and Walmart's price leadership position continues to resonate with customers in a challenging economy. Management raised its full-year earnings guidance, citing the underlying strength of its operations.
