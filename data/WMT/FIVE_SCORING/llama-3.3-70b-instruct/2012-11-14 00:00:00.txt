Walmart's Q3 earnings call highlighted the company's solid financial performance, with EPS increasing 11.3% year-over-year. The company delivered strong sales growth, with net sales up 3.4% to $113.2 billion, and operating income growing 4% to $6.1 billion. Walmart's U.S. segment saw a 1.5% comp sales increase, while Sam's Club posted a 2.7% comp sales increase without fuel. The company's international segment also performed well, with net sales up 2.4% to $33.2 billion. Overall, Walmart's strong financial results and solid execution position the company well for the holiday season.
