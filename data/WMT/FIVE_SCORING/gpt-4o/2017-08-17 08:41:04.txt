Walmart delivered solid Q2 results, showing strong growth in both U.S. comp sales and e-commerce, particularly with a 67% increase in U.S. GMV. Management expressed confidence in their innovative strategies and highlighted progress in international markets. Despite some margin pressures due to price investments and increased e-commerce mix, the upward revision in full-year EPS guidance and continued expansion of services like online grocery suggest a positive short-term outlook.
