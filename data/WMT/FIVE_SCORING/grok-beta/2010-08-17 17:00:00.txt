Walmart's Q2 FY 2011 earnings report reveals several positive developments for potential short-term stock price movement. Management has expressed satisfaction with profitability, raised full-year EPS guidance, and highlighted consistent earnings growth, strong shareholder returns through dividends and share repurchases, and improved return on investment despite challenging sales conditions. However, the cautionary tone on consumer behavior and a slightly tempered sales guidance may temper the enthusiasm.
