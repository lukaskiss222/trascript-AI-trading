UnitedHealth Group's earnings report showed strong performance in the fourth quarter and full year 2018, with revenue exceeding $226 billion and adjusted earnings per share growing 28% to $12.88. The company's guidance for 2019 remains unchanged, with expected adjusted net earnings per share in the range of $14.40 to $14.70. The report highlighted the company's diversified portfolio of businesses, including Optum and UnitedHealthcare, which are well-positioned for growth and innovation in the healthcare industry. Management expressed confidence in the company's ability to deliver solid operating and earnings performance in 2019 and beyond.
