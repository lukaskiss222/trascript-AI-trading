UnitedHealth Group's Q4 earnings call highlighted the company's strong performance, with modestly better-than-expected growth across its businesses. The company's guidance for 2017 remains unchanged, with projected revenues of $197-199 billion and adjusted net earnings of $9.30-9.60 per share. The acquisition of Surgical Care Affiliates (SCA) is expected to expand Optum's ambulatory care platform, providing further opportunities for growth. Management's tone was positive, emphasizing the company's commitment to its mission and its ability to adapt to changes in the healthcare market. Overall, the call suggested that UnitedHealth Group is well-positioned for continued growth and success in 2017.
