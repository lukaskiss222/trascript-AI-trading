UnitedHealth Group's earnings call revealed a strong finish to 2020, with results above expectations, driven by solid performances across Optum and UnitedHealthcare businesses. The company is optimistic about 2021, expecting continued growth, particularly in Medicare Advantage and Medicaid. Management's tone was positive, highlighting the company's efforts to build a high-performing healthcare system, innovate in areas like telehealth, and expand its value-based care offerings.
