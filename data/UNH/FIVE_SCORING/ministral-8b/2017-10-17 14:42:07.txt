### Analysis

The earnings call for UnitedHealth Group Incorporated revealed a strong performance across the company's various business segments in the third quarter of 2017. Key highlights include:

- **Revenue Growth**: UnitedHealth Group reported a 9% increase in revenues to $50.3 billion, driven by robust growth in both Optum and UnitedHealthcare.
- **Adjusted Earnings**: Adjusted earnings per share (EPS) rose by 23% to $2.66, with a total of $2.4 billion in earnings from operations.
- **Operating Costs**: The company managed to maintain a low operating cost ratio of 14.7%, reflecting efficient operations and cost management.
- **Medicare Advantage**: UnitedHealthcare's Medicare Advantage business saw a 17% increase in revenues, highlighting the strong performance and growth potential in this segment.
- **Optum**: Optum's revenues grew by 8.4% to nearly $23 billion, with earnings from operations increasing by 16%.
- **Optimism for 2018**: The management expressed optimism for continued growth in 2018, with expectations for adjusted earnings per share to approach $10.
