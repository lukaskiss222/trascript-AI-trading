UnitedHealth Group's Q4 2014 earnings call highlighted strong performance across various segments, exceeding expectations in nearly every area.  Management emphasized consistent growth in Medicaid and Medicare, despite some challenges in commercial and international markets.  The call pointed to a solid financial position with increased cash flow and a substantial share repurchase, indicating a strong stance for continued growth, including a projected 15-17% revenue increase in 2015.
