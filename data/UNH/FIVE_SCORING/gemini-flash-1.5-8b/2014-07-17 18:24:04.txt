UnitedHealth Group's earnings call indicates strong performance in the first half of 2014, exceeding expectations with raised revenue and earnings guidance.  Management highlighted robust growth in Medicaid and Medicare, along with positive Optum results.  While acknowledging some pricing pressures in commercial risk, the overall outlook for 2014 and into 2015 appears very positive, with the company projecting continued growth and a strong position in the healthcare landscape.
