UnitedHealth Group's Q1 2018 earnings call highlighted strong financial performance, exceeding expectations with higher revenues, adjusted cash flows, and earnings per share.  Management emphasized the company's strategic shift towards value-based care, with initiatives like Rally Care and OptumCare designed to increase efficiency and lower costs.  This, coupled with continued growth in Medicare Advantage and robust performance in Optum, points toward a positive short-term outlook for the stock.
