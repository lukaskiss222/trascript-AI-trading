The earnings call highlights the significant impact of the COVID-19 pandemic on UnitedHealth Group, particularly the reduction in elective procedures and potential impact on commercial group enrollment.  Management is maintaining its 2020 EPS outlook but acknowledges significant uncertainties and the need to adapt to the evolving situation.  The company is actively working to maintain service levels and address liquidity concerns for care providers, adjusting pricing strategies and potentially offering additional premium relief, to mitigate potential financial imbalances.
