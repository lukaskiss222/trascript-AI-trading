Johnson & Johnson's Q3 2024 earnings call highlights strong growth across multiple segments, particularly in Innovative Medicine with several key brands exceeding expectations and achieving significant regulatory milestones.  While there are some headwinds in MedTech, related to the Asia Pacific region,  management is confident in the company's strategy and increasing their guidance for the full year. The talc litigation resolution is progressing, but the timing remains uncertain.  This suggests a positive short-term outlook as strong growth across business segments and positive guidance will likely support a rally in the stock.
