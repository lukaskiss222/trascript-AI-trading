The management displayed confidence in sustaining momentum with their diversified product pipeline and upcoming launches. There is a focus on managing costs and leveraging their supply chain improvements. Given the positive earnings surprise, market position, and guidance maintaining a growth trajectory, the short-term impact on the stock is expected to be favorable.
