Johnson & Johnson's Q3 2018 earnings call highlighted strong performance across all segments, exceeding revenue and EPS expectations. The company raised its full-year guidance, reflecting confidence in continued growth. Pharmaceuticals saw robust sales led by oncology products, while the Consumer segment benefited from successful product launches like the Johnson's Baby relaunch. Although some challenges remain in Medical Devices, management outlined clear strategies for improvement. The positive financial results and optimistic outlook suggest a favorable short-term impact on the stock price.
