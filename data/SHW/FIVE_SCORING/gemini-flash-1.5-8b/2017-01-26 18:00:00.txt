Sherwin-Williams' earnings call highlighted strong Q4 performance, exceeding guidance in several key areas, particularly in the Paint Stores Group.  Management emphasized strong residential repaint activity and the positive impact of rising home values. While there are some concerns about raw materials and possible labor constraints, the outlook for 2017 is generally positive, with mid-single-digit sales growth anticipated.  The call suggests a potentially positive short-term stock impact as the company's performance surpassed expectations.
