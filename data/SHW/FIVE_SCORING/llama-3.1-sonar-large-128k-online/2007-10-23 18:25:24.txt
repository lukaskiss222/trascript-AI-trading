- **Positive Guidance**: Sherwin-Williams raised its expectations for diluted net income per share for the full year 2007 to a range of $4.70 to $4.75, indicating confidence in managing through upcoming challenges.
- **Share Buybacks and Dividends**: The company continued its practice of purchasing treasury stock and declared a higher quarterly dividend, which are positive signals for shareholders.
