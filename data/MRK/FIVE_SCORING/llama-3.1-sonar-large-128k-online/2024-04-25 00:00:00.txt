- Merck achieved robust growth with total worldwide sales of $15.8 billion, a 9% increase from Q1 2023, driven by strong demand for its innovative portfolio, particularly in oncology and vaccines[3][5").

### Key Product Performance
- KEYTRUDA sales grew 24% to $6.9 billion, driven by increased uptake in earlier-stage cancers and strong demand from metastatic indications[5].
