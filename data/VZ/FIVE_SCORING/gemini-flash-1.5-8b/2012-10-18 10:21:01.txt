Verizon's Q3 2012 earnings call highlighted strong performance, particularly in wireless with record-setting EBITDA service margins and accelerating postpaid net adds.  Management's confident tone and positive guidance for FiOS growth, coupled with a successful pension restructuring, suggest a positive outlook for the company.  However, potential headwinds from a declining and now less predictable postpaid upgrade rate and the anticipated impact of increased competition due to the anticipated regulatory changes, should be watched.
