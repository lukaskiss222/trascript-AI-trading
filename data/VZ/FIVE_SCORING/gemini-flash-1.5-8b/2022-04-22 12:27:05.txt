Verizon's Q1 2022 earnings call highlights strong 5G adoption and network expansion, particularly with C-Band deployments.  Management expressed confidence in the company's strategy and execution, projecting continued growth.  However, concerns about inflationary pressures and potential impacts on consumer spending and, to a lesser degree, some softening in postpaid phone gross adds in the latter part of the quarter, might influence investment sentiment in the short-term.
