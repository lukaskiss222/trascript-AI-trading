Verizon's Q3 2011 earnings call highlights strong Wireless performance, with record EBITDA margins and sustained growth in retail customers, revenue, and ARPU.  However, significant headwinds from severe storms and a labor strike affected Wireline results, impacting margins and installations.  Management anticipates a return to growth in Q4, aided by pent-up demand in FiOS and a promising new device launch.
