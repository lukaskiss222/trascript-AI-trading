Verizon's Q1 2017 earnings call highlights a positive response to their new Unlimited plan, leading to improved subscriber activity and decreased churn in the second half of the quarter.  While service revenue saw a decline due to the migration to new pricing plans, management is optimistic about the long-term trajectory, projecting revenue to improve in the second half of the year.  The call also emphasized continued network investment and strategic acquisitions, suggesting potential short-term pressures on revenue but a positive outlook for future growth.
