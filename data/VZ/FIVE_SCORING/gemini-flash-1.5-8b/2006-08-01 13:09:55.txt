Verizon's earnings call highlights continued strong performance in wireless, with record customer additions and low churn.  However, the wireline segment faces challenges from declining legacy revenue, but management emphasizes cost controls and the growth potential of fiber (FiOS) as a key driver of future profitability.  The company appears confident in its ability to offset wireline headwinds with strong wireless performance and expects ongoing margin improvement with cost reductions and strategic investments.
