Verizon's Q1 2006 earnings call highlights strong performance in wireless, driven by record net adds and low churn.  Management expressed confidence in continued margin expansion and synergy gains from the MCI merger, despite acknowledging challenges in the wireline segment.  The company's focus on broadband expansion (FiOS) and new products (video) suggests a positive outlook for long-term growth despite short-term wireline revenue declines.
