Verizon's Q3 2015 earnings call highlighted strong financial performance, driven by solid Wireless growth and increasing adoption of the device installment plan.  Management emphasized continued investment in network infrastructure and the launch of new platforms like go90, positioning the company for future growth in mobile video and the Internet of Things.  The call also addressed the transition to an unsubsidized device model, which is expected to impact earnings in the short term.
