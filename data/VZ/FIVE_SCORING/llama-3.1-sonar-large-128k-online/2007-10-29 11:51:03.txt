The third-quarter 2007 earnings call for Verizon Communications presents several key points that indicate a strong performance and positive outlook, despite some challenges.

### Key Highlights
- **Revenue Growth**: Consolidated revenues increased by 6% year-over-year to $23.8 billion, driven by strong wireless and FiOS growth.
- **Wireless Performance**: Verizon Wireless added 1.8 million retail net customers, with retail service revenues growing 15.9% and data revenues up 63% year-over-year.
- **FiOS Growth**: FiOS TV and Internet subscriptions saw significant growth, with 202,000 new TV customers and 229,000 new Internet customers added in the quarter.
- **Margin Expansion**: Operating income margins expanded to 18.1%, and EBITDA margin for wireless was 44.7%, indicating profitable growth.
- **Cash Flow and Share Repurchases**: Strong cash flows allowed for $800 million in share repurchases during the quarter, with a total of $1.7 billion year-to-date.
- **Guidance and Outlook**: The company raised its 2007 share buyback target to $2.5 billion and expressed confidence in its growth strategies, including continued investment in FiOS and wireless.

### Short-Term Implications
Given the strong performance across key segments, the positive guidance, and the company's ability to manage costs and expand margins, the short-term outlook for Verizon's stock is likely positive. Here are the main reasons:
- **Strong Revenue and Customer Growth**: The robust growth in wireless and FiOS services indicates a solid foundation for future revenue.
- **Margin Expansion**: Improved operating and EBITDA margins suggest increased profitability.
- **Share Repurchases and Dividend Increase**: These actions indicate confidence in the company's financial health and commitment to shareholder value.
- **Competitive Position**: Despite competition, such as from the iPhone, Verizon's wireless business showed minimal impact and strong customer retention.

### Conclusion
Considering the above points, the short-term impact on Verizon's stock price is expected to be positive.
