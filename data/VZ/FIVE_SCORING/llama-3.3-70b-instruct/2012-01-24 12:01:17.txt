Verizon's Q4 earnings call revealed strong revenue growth, driven by its Wireless segment, and a solid finish to 2011. The company's focus on 4G LTE, FiOS, and Enterprise Strategic Services contributed to significant improvements in adjusted revenue growth. Management expressed confidence in its ability to drive further penetration of smartphones and maintain its leadership position in 4G LTE. With a renewed focus on delivering solutions to customers and a strong financial position, Verizon is well-positioned for future growth and improved profitability.
