Verizon's Q3 2013 earnings call highlights a robust performance with strong revenue and earnings growth driven by its Wireless and FiOS segments. They reported an 8.4% increase in Wireless service revenue and a consolidation of its hold on Wireless market share, denoting strong customer retention and acquisition strategies. Management shared positive future outlooks, underscored by strategic acquisitions like Verizon Wireless and investment in 4G LTE capacity expansions, which are expected to fuel future growth. Any macroeconomic concerns affecting enterprise segments seemed tempered by Verizon's overall strategy and consistent execution, suggesting a favorable short-term impact on the stock.
