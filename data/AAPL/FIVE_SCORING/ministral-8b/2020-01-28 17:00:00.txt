The earnings call for Apple Inc. revealed a strong performance in the December quarter, with record revenue and earnings. The company's growth was driven by exceptional demand for the iPhone 11, 11 Pro, and 11 Pro Max, as well as robust performance in services, Wearables, and Mac. Despite the challenges posed by the coronavirus outbreak in China, Apple expects a wider than usual revenue range for the March quarter due to uncertainty related to the situation. The company also provided guidance for the March quarter, expecting revenue between $63 billion and $67 billion, with a gross margin between 38% and 39%. Overall, the earnings call highlighted Apple's strong position in the market and its ability to navigate challenges, which should have a positive impact on the stock price in the short term.
