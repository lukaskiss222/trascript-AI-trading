The earnings call for Apple Inc. reported strong financial results, with record revenues and margins. The company highlighted the success of the iPhone 6 and 6 Plus, the growth in emerging markets, and the positive reception of the Apple Watch. The management team provided guidance for the June quarter, expecting revenue to be between $46 billion and $48 billion, and gross margin to be between 38.5% and 39.5%. The company also announced an expansion of its capital return program to $200 billion by 2017, with a focus on share repurchases. The stock is likely to see a positive impact due to the strong results and the expansion of the capital return program. The rating is 1.
