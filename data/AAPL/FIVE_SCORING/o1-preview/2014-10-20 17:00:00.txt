Apple reported record-breaking Q4 results with strong revenue and EPS growth, fueled by high demand for the new iPhone 6 and 6 Plus. Management expressed optimism about future prospects, highlighting the successful launches of Apple Pay and Apple Watch, and noted strong performance in Mac sales. Despite some FX headwinds and a slight decline in iPad sales, the overall positive performance and upbeat guidance suggest the stock price will likely rise in the short term.
