Apple reported strong quarterly results, exceeding revenue expectations and demonstrating robust demand for the iPhone 7 and 7 Plus, with demand outpacing supply, especially for the 7 Plus. Services revenue grew by 24%, reaching a record $6.3 billion, and management expressed confidence in future growth, despite some concerns about China and gross margin pressures. Overall, the positive performance and optimistic outlook are likely to have a positive impact on the stock price in the short term.
