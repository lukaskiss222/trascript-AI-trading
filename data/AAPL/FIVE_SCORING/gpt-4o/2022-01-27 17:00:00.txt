Apple's Q1 FY 2022 earnings call highlighted its best quarterly revenue ever at nearly $124 billion, driven by strong performance across most product categories despite pandemic-related supply constraints. The company set records for iPhone, Mac, and services revenue, indicating robust customer demand and engagement. Management remains optimistic about overcoming supply issues and continuing growth, particularly with the iPhone and services sectors, supported by a significant increase in the customer base and paid subscriptions. The upbeat tone and record-breaking results suggest a short-term positive impact on Apple's stock price.
