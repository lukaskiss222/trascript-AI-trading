Apple Inc. reported a strong Q1 for fiscal year 2024 with revenue of $119.6 billion, which was a 2% increase compared to the previous year despite a shorter 13-week period. The company's EPS hit an all-time high of $2.18, representing a 16% increase. Tim Cook highlighted robust sales across various product lines, particularly the iPhone, and significant growth in emerging markets. The launch of the Apple Vision Pro has generated excitement, potentially driving near-term momentum. The company anticipates sustained double-digit growth in its services, despite challenges from a strong dollar. The positive earnings and strategic expansions are likely to have a favorable short-term impact on Apple's stock.
