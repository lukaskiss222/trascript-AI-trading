Apple reported a strong financial performance for Q1 Fiscal Year 2021, achieving record revenue of $111.4 billion and noting significant growth across all product categories and geographic segments. Key growth drivers included the iPhone 12 lineup, particularly the Pro and Pro Max models, and an expanding active installed base now exceeding 1 billion iPhones. Despite challenges from retail disruptions due to COVID-19, Apple achieved robust online sales and introduced innovations such as the M1 chip for Macs, which contributed positively to the company's performance. Management did not provide specific forward guidance but indicated optimism about the upcoming quarter.
