Apple Inc. delivered robust Q3 2015 results, with record revenue and earnings driven by strong iPhone sales and notable growth in Greater China, despite challenging foreign exchange conditions. CEO Tim Cook highlighted the successful Apple Watch launch, indicating a positive start, although specifics were not disclosed. Management expressed confidence in continued growth, driven by high customer satisfaction, expanding geographic reach, and new product innovations. This strong performance and optimistic future outlook are likely to positively influence the stock in the short term.
