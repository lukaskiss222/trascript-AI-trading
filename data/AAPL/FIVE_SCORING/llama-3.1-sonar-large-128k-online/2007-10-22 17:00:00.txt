- **Positive Guidance**: The company provided optimistic guidance for the December quarter, targeting revenue of about $9.2 billion and earnings per share of about $1.42, which is above street expectations.
- **International Growth**: Apple saw excellent growth in Europe and other international markets, which is expected to continue with the expansion of iPhone sales into new regions[6>.
- **Cash and Financial Health**: Apple ended the quarter with $15.4 billion in cash and no debt, indicating strong financial health.

Given these factors, the stock is likely to experience a positive impact in the short term as investors respond to the strong performance and optimistic outlook.
