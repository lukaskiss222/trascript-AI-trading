Apple's Q2 earnings call showcased strong revenue growth and record earnings, suggesting significant demand for their products, particularly Macs, iPhone, and upcoming 2.0 software. The anticipation around the iPhone's potential entry into new markets and the excitement around new product announcements indicate a positive short-term impact on stock price. However, the delay in recognizing iPhone revenue until the 2.0 software release might slightly temper this optimism.
