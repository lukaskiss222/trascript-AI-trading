Apple's Q3 FY09 earnings call revealed strong quarterly results, with record revenue and earnings. The company's Mac sales were robust, with 2.6 million units sold, and iPhone sales were particularly impressive, with 5.2 million units sold in the quarter. The new iPhone 3GS is constrained, but demand is strong, and the company is working to address supply issues. Overall, the call suggests a positive outlook for Apple, with management expressing enthusiasm for new products, including the upcoming Snow Leopard release.
