Apple's Q4 earnings call revealed strong performance, with record Mac sales and a new company record for iPhone sales. The company's guidance for the December quarter implies double-digit revenue growth, driven by the popularity of the iPhone 3GS and the success of the App Store. Management's tone was optimistic, highlighting the company's ability to innovate and deliver state-of-the-art products. While the company faces challenges in the economic environment, its focus on growing its ecosystem and expanding into new markets, such as China, positions it well for future growth. The stock is likely to react positively to the strong earnings report and guidance.
