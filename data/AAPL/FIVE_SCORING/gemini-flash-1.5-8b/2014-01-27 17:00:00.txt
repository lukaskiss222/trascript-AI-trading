Apple's Q1 2014 earnings transcript highlights record revenue and profits, driven by strong iPhone and iPad sales, particularly in emerging markets.  Management emphasizes strong customer satisfaction and loyalty, highlighting the continued importance of the iOS ecosystem, but cautious guidance indicates a potential short-term headwind.  The sequential decrease in revenue, partially related to inventory dynamics and currency impacts, suggests a temporary slowdown; however, the focus on innovation and developer ecosystem growth indicates longer-term growth potential.
