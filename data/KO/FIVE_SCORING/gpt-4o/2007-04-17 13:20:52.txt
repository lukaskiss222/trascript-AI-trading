Coca-Cola reported a strong start to 2007 with a 17% revenue increase and a 15% EPS growth, signaling positive momentum. The company continues to experience significant gains in international markets, particularly in emerging economies, while acknowledging challenges in the North American market. Management remains optimistic about addressing these issues through strategic innovation and partnerships, which should instill confidence amongst investors, likely leading to a positive impact on the stock price in the short term.
