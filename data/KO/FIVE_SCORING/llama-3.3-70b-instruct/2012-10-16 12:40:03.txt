The Coca-Cola Company's Q3 earnings call revealed positive results, with global volume growth of 4% and revenue growth of 6% despite facing macroeconomic headwinds. The company's still beverages portfolio grew 10% in the quarter, with strong performances in juice, juice drinks, and water. Management expressed confidence in meeting long-term growth targets and achieving double-digit operating profit growth in Q4. The new reporting structure and productivity initiatives are expected to drive further growth and efficiency.
