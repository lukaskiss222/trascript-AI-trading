Coca-Cola's Q4 2006 earnings transcript showcases strong global volume growth, particularly in international markets, with a focus on expanding core brands and innovating in non-carbonated beverages. However, there are concerns about North American performance, cost pressures from commodities, and a more challenging first half expected in 2007. This, along with some SG&A increase to boost brands, might temper investor sentiment in the short term.
