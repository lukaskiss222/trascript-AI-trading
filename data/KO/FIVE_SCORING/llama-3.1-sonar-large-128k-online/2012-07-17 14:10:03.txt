- Continued market share gains in nonalcoholic ready-to-drink beverages globally[4].
- Positive price mix and operating income growth, despite currency headwinds[4].

### Challenges and Cautionary Notes
- The global economy remains unpredictable, with macroeconomic uncertainties affecting consumer sentiment, especially in Europe and China[4].
- Gross margin pressure due to currency headwinds and changes in channel and package mix[4].
- Expected negative operating leverage in the third quarter due to higher SG&A spending and gross margin pressure[4].
- Currency impacts are expected to continue, with a significant headwind in the third quarter[4].

### Conclusion
Given the solid performance but acknowledging the ongoing macroeconomic challenges and short-term headwinds, the stock is likely to experience a neutral to slightly positive impact in the short term.
