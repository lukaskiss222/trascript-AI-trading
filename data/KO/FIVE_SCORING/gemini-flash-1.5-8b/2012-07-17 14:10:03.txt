Coca-Cola's Q2 2012 earnings transcript indicates continued solid performance despite global economic headwinds.  Management expressed confidence in meeting long-term growth targets, citing strong volume and revenue growth in several key markets (particularly China and India), and share gains in various beverage categories. While acknowledging challenges like a cooling Chinese economy and macroeconomic uncertainty, executives highlighted the importance of long-term strategies and investments in their global bottling system, positioning the company for continued success despite short-term fluctuations.
