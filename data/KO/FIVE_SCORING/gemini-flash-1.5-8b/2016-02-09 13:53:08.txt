Coca-Cola's earnings call highlighted accelerated refranchising plans, aiming for 100% by 2017.  This strategy, while potentially boosting long-term profitability and reducing risk, could create short-term headwinds related to divestiture costs and adjustments.  Management expressed confidence in the plan, emphasizing already-improved performance and market share gains, particularly in North America, suggesting a potentially positive short-term trajectory.
