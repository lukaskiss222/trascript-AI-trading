Coca-Cola's earnings call highlights significant near-term challenges due to the pandemic's impact on away-from-home consumption.  Management is projecting a substantial decline in Q2 volumes, but is focused on adapting to the changing environment through cost reductions, supply chain adjustments, revised marketing strategies, and an emphasis on e-commerce and value-oriented packaging.  This strategy focuses on short-term survival and flexibility to navigate an uncertain recovery.
