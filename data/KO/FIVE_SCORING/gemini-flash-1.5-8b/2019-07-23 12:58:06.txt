Coca-Cola's earnings call highlights robust organic revenue growth, driven by both developed and emerging markets, particularly in Asia Pacific.  Management expresses confidence in achieving full-year EPS targets despite currency headwinds.  The company's focus on portfolio expansion, including new products like Coke Energy, and initiatives to improve cash flow suggest potential for continued growth, albeit with some short-term concerns around currency fluctuations and execution in specific markets (Mexico, Argentina).
