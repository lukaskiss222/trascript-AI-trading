Procter & Gamble's Q1 earnings call revealed a strong start to the fiscal year, with organic sales growth of 4% and earnings per share above expectations. The company's innovation program and marketing investments are driving growth, with 7% organic volume growth and market share increases across most regions and brands. Although commodity costs and mix impacted gross margin, cost savings and operating efficiency improvements helped offset these pressures. Management maintained guidance for organic sales and earnings per share growth, and expressed confidence in their ability to deliver long-term value creation.
