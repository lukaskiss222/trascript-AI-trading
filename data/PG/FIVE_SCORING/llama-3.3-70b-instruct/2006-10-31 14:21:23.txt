Procter & Gamble's Q1 earnings call revealed a strong start to the fiscal year, with balanced top and bottom line growth driven by a healthy innovation program and strong operating margin improvement. The company's guidance for the fiscal year was raised, and the integration of Gillette is progressing well. While there are some concerns about commodity costs and private label pricing, the company's focus on innovation, brand equity, and trade-up programs is expected to drive growth.
