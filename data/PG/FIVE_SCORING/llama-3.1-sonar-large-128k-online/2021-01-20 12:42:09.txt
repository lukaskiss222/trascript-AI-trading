While some of these tailwinds may dissipate as the pandemic subsides, the company is well-positioned to maintain growth due to its strong brand portfolio and execution.
- **Cost Management and Productivity**: P&G has demonstrated strong cost management and productivity improvements, which have contributed to margin growth. The company is committed to continuing these efforts, which should support sustained profitability.
- **Capital Return**: P&G plans to return significant value to shareholders through dividends and share repurchases, totaling around $18 billion for fiscal year 2025, which is a positive signal for investors.
