- **Brand Strength**: The Nike brand, along with its affiliates like Converse and Hurley, showed robust growth, with Converse revenue up 32% and Hurley revenue up 38%[4].

- **Confident Outlook**: Management expressed confidence in their ability to manage through economic uncertainties and maintain high-single-digit revenue growth for the full year[4].
