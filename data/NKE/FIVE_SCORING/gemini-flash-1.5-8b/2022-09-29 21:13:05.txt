NIKE's Q1 earnings call indicated strong overall brand momentum and consumer demand, particularly in EMEA and APLA.  However, significant inventory concerns, especially in North America, due to earlier than expected retailer orders and improved transit times, are prompting aggressive inventory liquidation strategies.  Management anticipates this will have a transitory negative impact on gross margins this fiscal year, but the focus is on clearing space for new products and maintaining a strong foothold in the market.
