Nike's Q1 2013 earnings call highlighted strong revenue growth, driven by strong performance in the NIKE brand and Converse, especially in North America and emerging markets.  Management noted investments in innovation and consumer experiences, like the Olympics and Euro Championships, as key drivers. However, concerns over the pace of change in the Chinese market, inventory levels (especially for apparel), and the impact of currency fluctuations on gross margins emerged as possible near-term headwinds.
