Nike's Q1 2020 earnings call highlighted strong financial performance, exceeding expectations across various metrics.  Management expressed confidence in the company's growth strategy, particularly in digital channels and international markets,  and emphasized the positive impact of innovation and new product launches.  While FX headwinds and tariffs were acknowledged as impacting Q2, the overall tone suggests a positive outlook regarding the company's ability to manage these external factors and maintain short-term and long-term growth.
