Nike's Q3 2013 earnings call reveals a robust performance, with revenue up 9% on a reported basis and earnings per share growing by 20% to $0.73, thanks to strong sales across most regions and categories. The company's strategic focus on innovation, digital growth, and global market expansion, alongside cost management, paints an optimistic picture for the near future despite some challenges, particularly in the Chinese and Southern European markets. Management's confident and proactive tone in addressing these challenges suggests a positive short-term market reaction.
