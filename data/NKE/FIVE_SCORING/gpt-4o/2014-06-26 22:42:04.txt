Nike reported a strong fiscal 2014, with revenue growth of 10% to nearly $28 billion and an EPS increase of 11%, despite currency pressures. The management highlighted successful product innovations, enhanced digital strategies, and strong brand momentum, especially through significant events such as the World Cup. With guidance for high single-digit revenue growth and continued investment in digital and direct-to-consumer operations, the company's confidence in its growth strategy suggests a positive short-term outlook for the stock.
