### Analysis

NIKE, Inc. reported strong Q2 results with revenue growth of 5% and EPS of $0.46. The company highlighted significant growth in international markets, particularly Greater China and EMEA, driven by strong digital and comp store growth. The North American market showed signs of stabilization, with Nike Direct growth outpacing the consolidating wholesale marketplace. Management expressed confidence in the company's ability to drive sustainable growth through innovation, digital, and consumer experiences. The outlook for the second half of the fiscal year and fiscal year '19 is positive, with expectations for continued growth in international markets and a return to growth in North America.

### Conclusion

The short-term impact on the stock price is likely to be positive, driven by the company's strong Q2 results and positive outlook for the remainder of the fiscal year. The stock is expected to rally due to the company's focus on innovation, digital, and consumer experiences, which are likely to drive long-term growth.
