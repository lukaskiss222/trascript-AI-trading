Nike reported strong Q2 earnings with a 15% revenue increase to $7.4 billion and a 25% rise in EPS. Management expressed confidence in continued growth, highlighting investments in the women's segment and digital initiatives like e-commerce, which are expected to drive future performance. Despite some currency headwinds and macroeconomic challenges in specific markets, the positive results and upbeat guidance suggest the stock will have a positive short-term impact.
