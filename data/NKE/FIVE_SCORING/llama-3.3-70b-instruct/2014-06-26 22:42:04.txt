Nike's Q4 earnings call revealed a strong finish to fiscal 2014, with revenue growth of 10% to nearly $28 billion and diluted EPS up 11% to $2.97. The company's focus on innovation, consumer engagement, and digital transformation is driving growth, with notable successes in the women's and global football categories. Despite currency headwinds, Nike expects high single-digit revenue growth in fiscal 2015. The company's diversified portfolio and ability to manage risk position it for sustainable, profitable growth.
