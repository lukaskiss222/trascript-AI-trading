NIKE, Inc.'s Q1 fiscal 2008 earnings call revealed strong financial performance, with 11% revenue growth and 51% EPS growth. The company's international business drove sales, with China and Western Europe being notable standouts. Management expressed confidence in the company's growth plan, focusing on key categories and premium consumer experiences. While the U.S. market faced challenges, particularly in athletic specialty stores, Nike's diversified portfolio and strong brand position helped mitigate these issues.
