Nike's Q3 earnings call revealed a strong quarter with revenue growth, improved gross margins, and a clean inventory position. The company's focus on innovation, operational efficiency, and strategic investments in retail and digital platforms is paying off, with Nike Brand apparel revenues growing 3% and gross margins expanding 160 basis points. The company's premium positioning in China and growth in emerging markets also contributed to the strong results. With a solid balance sheet and $4 billion in cash, Nike is well-positioned for future growth and investment.
